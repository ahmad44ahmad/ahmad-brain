# خالد بن صالح الزهراني (Khalid bin Saleh Al-Zahrani)

## Identity correction (2026-04-23)

Earlier session memory split this person into two files:
- "Khalid Saleh" — Regional GM Al-Baha, grade 12 → 13
- "Khalid Al-Zahrani" — interim center supervisor (~3 months, formal assignment ended)

**Both refer to the same person: خالد بن صالح الزهراني.**

What actually happened (reconstruction from PDF + Ahmad's voice messages):
- Khalid Al-Zahrani built a multi-year track record at مركز التأهيل الشامل بالباحة (operational excellence: 4 administrations in 1 building, contractor-recovery, Najran-center hosting, sports teams, employment integration, etc.)
- During Al-Qarni's absence, he covered/expanded into broader regional roles
- As of 2026-04 he holds: **مدير عام (Director General) + مساعد لقطاع التنمية** simultaneously
- This is NOT a "covering" / "interim" position any more — it's a confirmed elevation

The "أمر إداري انتهى" comment from Ahmad (2026-04-22) likely referred to his INTERIM coverage of the center expiring because he was promoted UP to the larger regional + sector role.

## Kunya (clarified 2026-04-23)

**أبو فيصل** — his son is named Faisal. Ahmad uses "أبو فيصل" in speech/informal contexts. On formal documents use the full name "خالد بن صالح الزهراني" or the title "المدير العامّ للإدارة بمنطقة الباحة".

### Critical disambiguation (2026-04-23)

The kunya "أبو خالد" in Ahmad's earlier files refers to **Ali Al-Qarni** (Al-Qarni's son is named Khalid). It is NOT this person. Khalid Al-Zahrani's father's name is literally صالح — hence "بن صالح" — so he cannot be Al-Qarni's son. Two completely distinct people who happened to be previously conflated.

**Per Ahmad (2026-04-23, explicit):** stop writing about Al-Qarni or "Abu Khalid" going forward. That chapter is closed — Al-Qarni has departed from the center.

## Current role (as of 2026-04-23)

**مدير عام للإدارة بمنطقة الباحة + مساعد لقطاع التنمية** (Director General of Al-Baha regional admin + Assistant for Development Sector at HRSD).

**Clarification still pending:** Is "مساعد قطاع التنمية" a role within the regional admin (assistant who coordinates development-sector affairs at Al-Baha) OR a ministerial-level secondment (assistant to Deputy Minister السويلم)? Ask Ahmad.

## Career grade

Grade 12, targeting Grade 13. Reaching 13 triggers formal "مدير عام" rank designation (he holds the title now functionally; grade-13 confirmation makes it permanent rank).

## Track record (per Ahmad's strategic thesis PDF, written early 2026)

**Operational excellence at Al-Baha center under his leadership:**
- إدارة تعثر متعهد الإعاشة → built internal "العاجل من الأمور" rapid-response team
- تشغيل 4 إدارات في مبنى واحد رغم القصور الإنشائي
- ابتكار «الدليل المرئي للعناية الشخصية» — adopted ministry-wide
- تأسيس فرق رياضية (including women's football team in conservative environment) — won national medals
- حالات التوظيف والدمج المجتمعي بميزانية 17 ريال للمستفيد
- استضافة مركز نجران أثناء أزمة (likely Yemeni-border tensions)
- إدارة المخاطر والمرونة واستمرارية الأعمال
- الريادة في كفاءة الإنفاق وتحسين الأصول
- إعادة تعريف الجودة من مقاييس الخدمة إلى جودة الحياة

**Proactive leadership (سردية القيادة الاستباقية):**
- تمكين الموظفين في الخطوط الأمامية
- المشاركة من الأسفل-للأعلى في التحسين
- الابتكار المبني على الحاجة (Need-Driven Innovation) — submitted "أمن مستدام" to Ibtikarothon (but previous manager didn't forward it upward — see `people_ali_alqarni.md` for context)

- "حساب العائد على الاستثمار = ابتسامة الرضا على وجه المواطن"

**Transformative vision (per Ahmad's framing of Khalid):**

- Cross-sectoral collaboration mandatory (Health, Education, Municipalities/Housing)
- Al-Baha pilot → scalable model nationwide (with Al-Qassim mentioned as a target region in the PDF)
