# د. ناصر القحطاني (Dr. Nasser Al-Qahtani)

## Role

**وكيل الوزارة للتأهيل والتوجيه الاجتماعي** at MHRSD — current occupant of the position as of 2026.

## Why this memory matters

**Critical correction to earlier research:** Background agent research (2026-04-22) reported Dr. Abdullah Al-Wuhaibi as current وكيل for Rehabilitation & Social Guidance. Ahmad corrected this explicitly on 2026-04-23:
- Al-Wuhaibi was wakil at the time he signed the center's Excellence Certificate in 2022
- Al-Wuhaibi has since moved on
- **Dr. Nasser Al-Qahtani now holds the position**

This correction is load-bearing for Basira handover correspondence — the primary formal-owner addressee must be Al-Qahtani, not Al-Wuhaibi.

## Known facts (so far)

- Title: وكيل الوزارة للتأهيل والتوجيه الاجتماعي
- Appointed: year not captured — some time after Al-Wuhaibi's tenure ended (Al-Wuhaibi signed the 2022 Excellence cert; Al-Qahtani replaced him in an unknown year, definitively in place by 2026)
- Owns: 36 (or 38 per Ahmad's current count) comprehensive rehabilitation centers across KSA + disability services + orphans + elderly + kafalah + ihtiwa

## Relevance to Basira

On any Basira referral letter, Al-Qahtani is:
- **Formal domain owner** — the wakala he leads is the site of Basira's use-case
- **Appropriate recipient of a نسخة** (copy), not primary addressee (primary goes to Al-Ghamdi per `feedback_basira_governance_layer.md`)
- Not the path-to-scale (that's via Al-Ghamdi's Shared Services due to Quality GA ownership + sector-mandate from Minister)

## Gaps

- Year of appointment.
- Prior role / background.
- Direct office contact.
- Whether he is aware of the center's existing track record (Excellence certs were signed by his predecessor — but the recognition is institutional, not personal to Al-Wuhaibi).
