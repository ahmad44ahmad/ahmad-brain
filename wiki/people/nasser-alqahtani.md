# Dr. Nasser Al-Qahtani — د. ناصر القحطاني

## Role

**وكيل الوزارة للتأهيل والتوجيه الاجتماعي** — Deputy Minister for Rehabilitation & Social Guidance at MHRSD. Current occupant of the position as of 2026.

## Identity correction (2026-04-23)

A prior background-agent research run (2026-04-22) reported **Dr. Abdullah Al-Wuhaibi** as current Deputy Minister. Ahmad corrected this explicitly on 2026-04-23:

- Al-Wuhaibi was Deputy Minister at the time he signed the center's Excellence Certificate in **2022**.
- Al-Wuhaibi has since moved on.
- **Dr. Nasser Al-Qahtani now holds the position.**

This correction is **load-bearing** for Basira handover correspondence — the primary formal-owner addressee must be Al-Qahtani, not Al-Wuhaibi.

## Known facts

- Title: وكيل الوزارة للتأهيل والتوجيه الاجتماعي.
- Appointed: year not captured — some time after Al-Wuhaibi's tenure ended (Al-Wuhaibi signed the 2022 Excellence cert; Al-Qahtani replaced him in an unknown year, definitively in place by 2026).
- Portfolio: 36 (or 38 per Ahmad's current count) Comprehensive Rehabilitation Centers across KSA + disability services + orphans + elderly + kafalah + ihtiwa.

## Basira routing relevance

On any Basira referral letter, Al-Qahtani is:

- **Formal domain owner** — the wakala he leads is the site of Basira's use-case.
- **Appropriate recipient of a نسخة (copy)**, not primary addressee. Primary addressee is [[ismail-alghamdi]] per the governance layer rule (Al-Ghamdi holds the Quality portfolio + Minister-assigned Development Sector mandate).
- **Not the path-to-scale** — that's via Al-Ghamdi's Shared Services wakala.

with two assistant-agencies underneath (الأسرة والتَوجيه الاجتماعي,
التَأهيل الاجتماعي).

Two scenarios:
1. **Position vacant / between appointments.** Likely if there has been
   organizational churn since 2026-04-23 (when Ahmad confirmed Al-Qahtani
   was current).
2. **Position filled but not yet published on the public org-chart page.**
   The page is dynamically rendered ("Loading…" placeholders are visible
   for several positions even on direct fetch).

**Until verified:** Treat Al-Qahtani as the **most recent confirmed**
holder, not the **definite current** holder. Any Basira correspondence
should:
