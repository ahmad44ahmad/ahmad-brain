# Dr. Nasser Al-Qahtani — د. ناصر القحطاني

## Role

**وكيل الوزارة للتأهيل والتوجيه الاجتماعي** — Deputy Minister for Rehabilitation & Social Guidance at MHRSD. Current occupant of the position as of 2026.

## Identity correction (2026-04-23)

A prior background-agent research run (2026-04-22) reported **Dr. Abdullah Al-Wuhaibi** as current Deputy Minister. Ahmad corrected this explicitly on 2026-04-23:

- Al-Wuhaibi was Deputy Minister at the time he signed the center's Excellence Certificate in **2022**.
- Al-Wuhaibi has since moved on.
- **Dr. Nasser Al-Qahtani now holds the position.**

This correction is **load-bearing** for Basira handover correspondence — the primary formal-owner addressee must be Al-Qahtani, not Al-Wuhaibi.

- Appointed: year not captured — some time after Al-Wuhaibi's tenure ended (Al-Wuhaibi signed the 2022 Excellence cert; Al-Qahtani replaced him in an unknown year, definitively in place by 2026).
