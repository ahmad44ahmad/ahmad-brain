# Ismail bin Saeed Al-Ghamdi — إسماعيل بن سعيد الغامدي

## Why this note matters

Per Ahmad's explicit strategic assessment (2026-04-22), Al-Ghamdi is the **single strongest patron** for routing Basira through the ministry. His combined positioning:

- **Tech fluency:** ex-Chairman of Mobily (Saudi telecom), previously at Microsoft. Understands digital architecture + procurement at the level Basira operates. A non-technical Deputy Minister would need translation; Al-Ghamdi does not.
- **Ecosystem position:** Assistant Minister for Shared Services (مساعد وزير للخدمات المشتركة) + heads الإدارة العامة للجودة (Quality GA). The Minister later assigned him قطاع التنمية (Development Sector) — multi-hat authority.
- **Existing relationship with Al-Baha center:** personally received the **1st-Arab Quality Award** (UAE event) **on behalf of** the Al-Baha Comprehensive Rehab Center. Per Ahmad: *"هو الذي استلم الجائزة"*. This is **not a cold contact**.
- **Vision alignment:** Minister Ahmad Al-Rajhi's framing — *"نستهدف أن نكون الأول على العالم"* — aligns naturally with a patron whose track record is quality excellence.

## Verified career facts (ministry research 2026-04-22)

- **Full name:** الأستاذ إسماعيل بن سعيد الغامدي / Ismail bin Saeed Al-Ghamdi.
- **Previous role 1:** Chairman of Mobily (Etihad Etisalat) — Saudi telecom operator. (An earlier memory said "CEO" — corrected to Chairman per ministry research.)
- **Previous role 2:** Microsoft KSA — head of the Education portfolio (not just generic Microsoft — ran the education vertical).
- **Previous role 3:** Cisco — earlier technology career.
- **Current role (as of 2026-04-22):** مساعد وزير الموارد البشرية والتنمية الاجتماعية للخدمات المشتركة — Assistant Minister for Shared Services. Royal Order appointment **Shaaban 1444 AH / March 2023 CE**.
- **Rank note (critical):** مرتبة ممتازة (Assistant Minister rank) — in Saudi protocol, this is **senior** to وكيل وزارة (Deputy Minister). Al-Ghamdi reports directly to Minister Ahmad Al-Rajhi, bypassing the Deputy-Minister tier.
- **Known initiative:** launched **HRSD Elite** — senior-leadership development program partnered with Harvard.
- **Sector ownership:** Shared Services wakala contains Quality (الإدارة العامة للجودة), internal HR, facilities, HRSD Elite. Holds the Quality portfolio directly.
- **Minister-assigned extension:** قطاع التنمية (Development Sector) was mandated to him by Minister's order — gives him hierarchical authority over [[nasser-alqahtani]]'s wakala (وكالة التأهيل والتوجيه الاجتماعي) through sector oversight.
- **Known award context:** personally received the **1st-Arab Quality Award** at a UAE ceremony on behalf of مركز التأهيل الشامل بالباحة. **Exact year and award name: still to verify with Ahmad.**
