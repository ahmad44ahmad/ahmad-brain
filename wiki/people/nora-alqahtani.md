# Nora A. Alqahtani — نورة القحطاني

## Role
Officer at the **General Admin for Social Quality (الإدارة العامة للجودة الاجتماعية)**, ministry central.

## Why she matters
Co-originator (with [[hanoof-alelayan]]) of the **74-indicator periodic monitoring report on services for persons
with disabilities** (تقرير المتابعة الدورية لمعايير خدمات الأشخاص ذوي الإعاقة) — a kingdom-wide quarterly task across
all CRC centers. Ahmad is the Al-Baha point of contact who fills and closes the indicator file each quarter (Q1/Q2
submitted; urgent reminder 2025-11-18 with a 2025-11-19 deadline; Q4-2025 submitted "تم إكمال المهمة" 2026-02-04).
She is the central task-owner he reports to on this thread.
