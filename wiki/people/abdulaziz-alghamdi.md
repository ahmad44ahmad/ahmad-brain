# Abdulaziz A. Alghamdi — عبدالعزيز الغامدي

## Role
**أخصائي التسكين** (manpower / fingerprint-placement specialist) at مركز التأهيل الشامل بالباحة. Issues
center-wide HR circulars and is the operational enforcer of attendance and leave rules.

- **Email:** `a.a.alghamdi024@hrsd.gov.sa`
- **Volume:** ~37 messages to Ahmad, 2025-03 → 2026-02 (one of the highest-volume correspondents).

## Why he matters
He is the **sender of the recurring "explain your absence" demands and the fingerprint-placement (تسكين) notices**
that are the trigger of the [[absence-deduction-dispute]]. He is an operational counterpart, not an adversary —
the dispute is with the system and the deductions, not with him personally.
