- **Email:** `a.a.alghamdi030@hrsd.gov.sa`

## Why he matters
The HR counterpart on Ahmad's grievance arc:
- Recipient of the **training-neglect grievance** (2025-08-16, 4–5 attachments) — Grievance C.
- Recipient of the long candid reform letter **"حديثٌ من القلب للموارد: عتابُ مُحب، ومكاشفةُ مهني، ورؤيةٌ لما هو آت"** (2026-02-10, 13 attachments), pushing job-placement-on-actual-title (التسكين) per the Hamad Al-Rumaih circular.

The Branch DG referred the absence matter back to him on 2026-02-23.

Creating this note **closes one of the two "No vault note yet" danglers** in [[overtime-grievance-case]].
