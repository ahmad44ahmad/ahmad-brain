# HRSD Performance Management System

The MHRSD's official **annual employee performance-appraisal system** (`نظام الأداء الوظيفي`, latest version),
operated inside the ministry HR platform `نظام موارد`. It is the formal instrument through which Ahmad's manager
[[ali-alqarni]] (employee ID 8479) records and approves his performance. Full document harvest (17 docs, the
system mechanics) lives on the Desktop at `HRSD-Performance-System-Harvest/` (English KB; this note is the vault distillation).

## The charter (الميثاق) — three pillars
Each employee's approved charter rests on three weighted pillars, plus feeders:
1. **Objectives (`الأهداف`)** — 4–5 objectives, each weight `≥ 10%`, summing to **100%**. Fields include
   `معيار المقياس · الناتج المستهدف · نوع حساب المقياس (عدد/نسبة، تصاعدي/تنازلي) · الوزن النسبي · ارتباط الهدف بالاستراتيجية`.
   A strategic objective cascades from the manager (employee 90% + manager strategic 10%, or employee 100% if dropped).
   Objectives **lock after `اعتماد`**.
2. **Behavioural competencies (`الجدارات`)** — weighted to 100%, rated 1–5 on `مستوى الإتقان` (five ascending mastery
   rungs) by the direct manager against `المؤشرات السلوكية`. Built on the **Saudi Unified Classification of
   Occupations (دليل التصنيف السعودي للمهن, ISCO-08)**. Weights are manager-set except `كفاءة الإنفاق` (uniform).
   For Ahmad's occupation **`أخصائي علاج طبيعي`** the five core competencies and weights are: `المسؤولية 20 ·
   العمل الجماعي 20 · المرونة للتغيير 20 · المبادرة 25 · كفاءة الإنفاق 15`.
3. **Knowledge-transfer (`الوثيقة الداعمة لنقل المعرفة`)** — evidence pillar (`الممارسة المطبقة · الجهة المستفيدة ·
   فترة التنفيذ`); mandatory for contractors and formal supervisors, and also exists as a weighted manager objective.

Feeders: **Tasks (`المهام`)** roll up under a parent objective; **Achievements (`الإنجازات`)** are logged in a
separate register. A confidential **Skills & Aspirations questionnaire (`المهارات والرغبات الوظيفية`)** is *not scored*
(the manager cannot see it) and feeds development + job-rotation routing only.

## The grading scale (نطاقات التقييم) — 5 bands
`5 مثالي / أزرق / >100%` · `4 تخطى التوقعات / تركواز / >90–100%` · `3 وافق التوقعات / أخضر / 80–90%` (the default) ·
`2 بحاجة إلى تطوير / أصفر / 60–<80%` · `1 غير مرضي / برتقالي / <60%`. The distribution is enforced by a
**normal-distribution curve** (`منحنى التوزيع الطبيعي`, ~70% expected in `وافق التوقعات`); the higher manager can
re-seat bands. Legal basis: `المادة الثالثة`. Benchmarked to Aramco and IBM.

## The load-bearing rule: how the top band is earned
Grades map from **achievement-of-target**: hitting **100%** of target → grade 4 (`تخطى التوقعات`); **80%** → grade 3
(`وافق التوقعات`). **Grade 5 (`مثالي`) is NOT reached by meeting targets** — it requires **exceeding 100% through
developmental / creative achievements (`إنجازات تطويرية/إبداعية`)** logged in the Achievements register. This makes
the Achievements register the *only* path to a 5 — which is why a portfolio of high-priority approved achievements is
load-bearing for the top band.

## The Achievements register (الإنجازات)
A standalone register for accomplishments beyond the formal objectives. Per-row: description · date · link ·
**priority (`أولوية عالية جداً = 5`, on a 1–5 scale)** · **category (`مشاركة أو عضوية في لجان` / `نقل معرفة`)** ·
status. Two-state workflow: `تم إدخال الإنجاز من قبل الموظف → تم اعتماد الإنجاز`. This register is the source of
[[ahmad-2025-achievements]] — Ahmad's **ten 2025 achievements, all rated 5/`أولوية عالية جداً`**, approved by
[[ali-alqarni]] (raw export: [[anjazati-pms-xlsx-2026-06-05]]). In the system's own logic, those ten are the
developmental achievements that substantiate a `مثالي` outcome.

## Cycle & controls
- **Annual cycle (2025):** charter registration + approval 09/03→24/04; mid-year edit-window 10/08→21/08 + `الإفادة
  النصف سنوية` 24/08→28/08; year-end evaluation 16/11→18/12; system close 18/12. The mid-year is a **checkpoint**, not
  a separately scored grade.
- **Probation:** `< 3 months` tenure → no evaluation. **Grievances:** a 14-day `تظلم` window; an employee graded
  `مثالي` is excluded from filing. Identity data is pulled from `نظام موارد`; the source docs are classified `مقيّد`.
