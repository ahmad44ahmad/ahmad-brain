# MHRSD Leadership Map — 2026

Verified via background research 2026-04-22, corrected against Ahmad's own account 2026-04-23. Minister + Assistant Minister (مرتبة ممتازة — senior to وكلاء) + seven deputy ministers.

## Minister

**م. أحمد بن سليمان الراجحي** — وزير الموارد البشرية والتنمية الاجتماعية. Appointed June 2018, permanent. Vision framing: *"نستهدف أن نكون الأول على العالم"* — matters for Basira's sovereign deck framing.

## Assistant Minister (مرتبة ممتازة — senior to وكلاء)

**الأستاذ إسماعيل بن سعيد الغامدي** — مساعد الوزير للخدمات المشتركة. Royal Order Shaaban 1444 / March 2023. Full profile: [[ismail-alghamdi]].

Shared Services portfolio covers:

- الإدارة العامة للجودة (Quality General Administration — the ISO 9001 home)
- HRSD Elite (Harvard partnership)
- Internal HR
- Facilities
- Governance functions

Al-Ghamdi is Basira's primary patron — Al-Baha's First-Arab Quality Award was received by him on the center's behalf.

## Deputy Ministers (وكلاء الوزارة)

### 1. د. عبدالله بن ناصر أبوثنين — Labor

وكيل الوزارة للعمل. Pre-2023, permanent. Owns the labour-market side. "إدارة الامتثال" in HRSD vocabulary lives here (employer-side compliance — Tamam, inspection) — **not** the digital-system regulatory-conformance function.

### 2. أ. بدر بن إبراهيم السويلم — Social Development

نائب وزير الموارد البشرية والتنمية الاجتماعية للتنمية الاجتماعية (قطاع التنمية). Royal Order 12 February 2026. Background: CPA / SOCPA, GCCBDI; previously SEC, LIVA, MEFIC REIT boards.

> ⚠️ User memory (2026-04-22) describes a "<1 week old" new appointment in the Development sector. Al-Suwailem is ~10 weeks prior to that note; Ahmad may be referring to a DG-level (not DM) newer appointment — verify.

### 3. م. فيصل بن أحمد باخشوين — Digital Transformation

وكيل الوزارة للتحول الرقمي. Since 2020, permanent. Won "Best Digital Transformation Leader 2022" (DGA award). MHRSD placed **#1 in the DGA Digital Transformation Index 2025** — he actively protects this ranking.

> 🚨 **Risk flag for Basira.** DT wakalas historically slow-walk externally-built systems ("not built by us, must be re-platformed on our stack"). Do NOT make Bakhshwain the primary addressee. Use as نسخة *after* Al-Ghamdi sponsorship is locked.

### 4. أ. رائد بن عبدالعزيز الجرباء — Beneficiary Experience & Branches

وكيل الوزارة لتجربة المستفيد والفروع. Since 2022. The wakala itself was established in 2019 as **the first dedicated beneficiary-experience wakala in Saudi government**. Alignment with Basira is medium — Basira is decision-support for leadership, not directly beneficiary-journey UI. Relevant only when beneficiary-facing modules ship.

### 5. أ. راشد بن محمد الجلاجل — Strategic Affairs & Vision Realization

وكيل الوزارة للشؤون الاستراتيجية وتحقيق الرؤية. Since October 2019. Owns Vision 2030 alignment + National Transformation Program (NTP) tracking. Also Secretary-General of Sakan Foundation (secondary role).

Alignment with Basira: long-term. **He is the exit-to-scale, not the entry door.** If Basira becomes an NTP initiative, it flows through him.

### 6. د. ناصر القحطاني — Rehabilitation & Social Guidance *(Basira's domain owner)*

وكيل الوزارة للتأهيل والتوجيه الاجتماعي. Current occupant. Full profile: [[nasser-alqahtani]].

> ⚠️ **Correction to external agent research (2026-04-22) that named Al-Wuhaibi as current.** Per Ahmad 2026-04-23: Al-Wuhaibi WAS wakil in 2022 (his signature is on Al-Baha's Excellence Certificate), but has moved on. Al-Qahtani replaced him. Al-Wuhaibi remains historically significant — address current correspondence to Al-Qahtani.

Owns 36–38 rehab centres, disability services, orphans, elderly, kafalah, ihtiwa. **Basira's formal domain owner — always include him as نسخة on formal correspondence.**

### 7. (وكيل الوزارة للموارد البشرية — internal HR wakala)

Exists as a wakala but the specific deputy name was not publicly locatable at research time. Internal ministry HR, distinct from the Labor wakala.

### Terminology warnings

- **"إدارة الامتثال"** at HRSD publicly refers to **employer-side labour-market compliance** (Tamam, ma3an, inspection). It does NOT refer to digital-system regulatory conformance. For Basira-style reviews the correct term is **"الحوكمة والمطابقة"** under Quality or Internal Audit.
- **"الأمن السيبراني"** is NOT the internal unit name. Use **"إدارة أمن المعلومات"** in correspondence — NCA ECC controls are applied, but the unit title remains Information Security.

## How to use this map

### Primary Basira referral letter

- **Primary addressee:** Al-Ghamdi (Shared Services) — conventional first door and strategically advantageous (pro-digital-innovation track record, patron relationship with Al-Baha via UAE award).
- **Copy #1:** Al-Qahtani (Rehab & Social Guidance) — domain owner. Ensure Al-Wuhaibi-era endorsements are cited, but address Al-Qahtani.
- **Copy #2:** Bakhshwain (Digital Transformation) — protect against turf objection.
- **Do NOT address as primary:** Al-Suwailem (too new), Al-Julajel (strategic exit, not entry), Al-Jarba (wrong domain alignment yet).

### Post-cybersecurity scaling

Once Basira has Quality + Information Security clearance:

- **Al-Julajel** (Strategic Affairs) becomes the natural next patron for cross-wakala scaling and NTP registration.
- **Al-Jarba** (Beneficiary Experience) becomes relevant when beneficiary-facing modules ship.
