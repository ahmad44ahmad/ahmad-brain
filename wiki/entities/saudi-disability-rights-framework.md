# Saudi Disability-Rights Framework

> **Verification discipline (inherited from the framework note):** `[verified]` = read in a primary/official source or two agreeing sources; `[verify]` = single secondary mirror / unconfirmed; ⚠️ = sources conflicted. Fabricate nothing; assert no `[verify]` number as fact. Research date 2026-06-10.

## The authority (APD)

**Saudi Authority for the Care of Persons with Disabilities (هيئة رعاية الأشخاص ذوي الإعاقة / APD)** — established by **Council of Ministers decision No. 266, dated 27/5/1439H** `[verified]`. Mandate: care for PWDs, secure their rights, enhance other agencies' services. This is the licensing/inspection regulator already named in [[basira-capability-domain-catalog]]; this note is its dedicated home (no separate `[[apd]]` stub). Site: https://apd.gov.sa/en/about-us

## The governing instrument

**نظام حقوق الأشخاص ذوي الإعاقة (Rights of Persons with Disabilities Law)** — promulgated by **Royal Decree No. (م/27) dated 11/2/1445H (27/8/2023)**, approving **Cabinet Resolution No. 110 dated 6/2/1445H**, published **23/2/1445H (8/9/2023)**, in force **120 days after publication** `[verified]`. It **replaced** the prior **نظام رعاية المعوقين (Disability Welfare Law), Royal Decree No. (م/37), 23/9/1421H (19 Dec 2000)**; conflicting provisions cancelled `[verified]`. **Cite the 2023 law, not the 2000 one** — the deprecated workshop deck is the 2000 era.
- BOE record: https://laws.boe.gov.sa/BoeLaws/Laws/Viewer/e3923599-e921-4891-bed2-b07d00e4fd4b
- Library of Congress: https://www.loc.gov/item/global-legal-monitor/2023-12-03/saudi-arabia-new-law-regulating-rights-of-persons-with-disabilities-promulgated/

## The de-institutionalization spine (international backdrop)

No explicit DI mandate was located in the Saudi law `[verify]` (absence-from-secondary, not verified absence — never assert as fact). The Saudi instrument supplies **entitlements**; the **DI principle** rests on CRPD:
- **CRPD Art. 19** (live independently / included in community — choice of residence, range of community supports) `[verified]`: https://www.un.org/development/desa/disabilities/convention-on-the-rights-of-persons-with-disabilities/article-19-living-independently-and-being-included-in-the-community.html
- **General Comment No. 5 (2017)** — DI is structural reform beyond closing institutions; "family-like" institutions are still institutions `[verified]`.
- **CRPD/C/5 — 2022 DI Guidelines** `[verified]`: https://www.ohchr.org/en/documents/legal-standards-and-guidelines/crpdc5-guidelines-deinstitutionalization-including
- **CRPD Art. 12 + General Comment No. 1 (2014)** — shift to supported decision-making; legal capacity ≠ mental capacity `[verified]`. Dismantles "he can't decide so he must stay."
- KSA CRPD ratification — widely reported but `[verify]` (confirm via OHCHR before citing).

## Replacing the deficit model (Topic 2)

- **AAIDD 12th-edition supports paradigm** (Schalock/Luckasson/Tassé, 2021) — ID = limitations in intellectual functioning AND adaptive behavior; assess **support needs**, do not sort by IQ `[verified]`. Replaces educable/trainable.
- **Schalock & Verdugo 8-domain QoL** as the outcome framework `[verified]`.
- **SDLMI** (Wehmeyer et al. 2000; Shogren & Wehmeyer) — evidence-based self-determination instruction; causal impact on goal attainment `[verified]`; exact phase names `[verify]`.
- **WHO ICF** biopsychosocial model `[verify]` (cite WHO directly before official use).

These instruments are operationalized in [[basira-capability-domain-catalog]]. **Confidence is `medium`** because every article number above 16 and the DI-absence finding are unverified.
