# Al-Baha CRC — Physical Plant & Infrastructure

## What this note is

The physical-plant sibling of [[albaha-center-org]]: that note holds the people/structure; this holds the building, systems, and AS-IS engineering condition. Distilled from a 14-agent vision harvest of Ahmad's photographic archive (2026-06-05). The source carries a property deed, owner/municipal-official names, per-vendor priced quotations, and vendor CR/VAT numbers — **all stripped here per the PII gate**; the raw archive stays local on Desktop and never enters the vault.

## Identity & site

- Consultant **KHOZAM / SAPAC**; **2010 design** (AS-BUILT drawings exist).
- Plot **6,888.81 m²**; perimeter wall ~738.6 m; registered deed (number withheld).
- **G+2**, three functional blocks plus shared utility rooms.
- *Source caveat (do not silently resolve):* two building-permit area schedules in the archive **do not reconcile** — neither is treated as canonical.

## The three blocks + shared utility rooms

- **Men's block** (largest) — bedrooms A1–A13, dental, pharmacy, recreation.
- **Women's block** — dorms 1–10, PT unit, mother-&-child, isolation. (The center is a **women's residential** facility in QMS framing; the men's block reflects the full physical archive.)
- **Administrative block.**
- **Shared:** main electrical room, transformer room (4), generator room (2), underground fire-pump room, guard room.

## AS-IS infrastructure condition (Oct–Nov 2022 inspection campaign)

A civil-defense-driven safety/maintenance inspection by the main contractor + specialist vendors. Per system:

| System | AS-IS condition (2022) |
|---|---|
| **Fire alarm** | Zeta panel (men's/admin) with one dead; Contex Plus (women's). *8-vs-16-zone reading conflict flagged in source.* |
| **Fire fighting** | 500 GPM; **NO diesel fire pump** → recommend an ATS-tied backup pump. |
| **CCTV** | ~94 installed + ~30 dismantled. |
| **Electrical** | 4× UTEC 1250 KVA oil transformers — all need oil-sample lab testing. |
| **Standby generators** | 610 KW (Mitsubishi-type) + 415 KW (Detroit Diesel) — both need service. *Supplier numbering conflict flagged.* |
| **Elevator** | Fuji, 3–4 stops. |
| **HVAC** | ~133–168 split units; room-by-room snag list, widespread "no cooling". *List incomplete — flagged.* |

There is also a fixed-plant asset register (models/serials — equipment IDs, not personal data, kept). The 2022 repair quotations total an aggregate **~SAR 400,895** (per-vendor priced line-items + CR/VAT numbers omitted per the PII gate).
