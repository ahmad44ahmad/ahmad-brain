# Social-Handicap Compass

## The core principle

> **الخطيئة:** الانجراف إلى التشخيص الطبي ونسيان العوق الاجتماعي.
> **التصحيح:** العَوق الطبّي مسؤولية المستشفى. عمل أحمد في الطبقة الأعلى — **تفكيك ما يُعيق الإنسان اجتماعيًا**، ليس علاج جسده.

Medical disability (العَوق الطبّي) is a hospital responsibility. Ahmad's work — quality, institutional engineering, [[basira]], [[pt-modeling]], the career portfolio — operates **one layer above the medical layer**. Every proposal must be able to answer two questions: which type of social handicap are we dismantling, and is our solution dismantling it or reproducing it?

## The compass dial — ten types of social handicap

| # | Type | Nature | Example in Ahmad's context |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **عائق** (barrier) | Physical, value-neutral | Missing ramp, narrow gate |
| 2 | **تحدٍّ** (challenge) | Active friction | Bureaucracy of approvals |
| 3 | **خصم** (opponent) | Declared opposition | Staff member who says *"لا يستحقون"* |
| 4 | **مضادّ** (antagonist) | Systemic force | Zero-sum institutional culture |
| 5 | **داعم مزيّف** (fake supporter) | Signs without executing | A deputy who signs off and then freezes the file |
| 6 | **مصدر خوف** (source of fear) | Stigma generator | The village café that labels the family |
| 7 | **خوف** (fear) | Internalised | A beneficiary who refuses to leave the house |
| 8 | **عُرف اجتماعي** (social norm) | Unwritten expectation | *"الرعاية في البيت بواسطة الأسرة"* — care at home by the family |
| 9 | **ثقافة تابو** (taboo) | What cannot be said | Sexuality, marriage, income effects |
| 10 | **مُسلَّم ديني** (religious given) | Unquestioned dogma | *"إعاقة ابتلاء"* — disability as ordeal, as a ceiling on discussion |

### Extensions (optional slots)

- **التوقّع المنخفض** (soft bigotry) — *"لا تُرهقه فهو معاق"*.
- **المعرفة المفقودة** (missing measurement) — *"ما لا يُقاس، لا يُدار"*.
- **السيطرة الإحسانية** (charity dominance) — *"مسكين"* erasing *"مُواطن"*.

## How to apply before any project, proposal, or document

Three mandatory questions. If any is absent or self-contradictory, the compass is off — **pause and reframe before executing**.

1. **أيّ نوع عوق نستهدف؟** Which of the ten types (or extensions) are we targeting?
2. **هل الحلّ يُفكّكه أم يُعيد إنتاجه؟** Is the proposed solution dismantling it, or reproducing it under a new label?
3. **هل نحن في دور المستشفى (طبّي) أم في دور أحمد (اجتماعي)؟** Are we in the hospital's role (medical) or Ahmad's role (social)?

## Recompass triggers

Nine patterns that signal drift back into the medical frame. When a pattern appears in Ahmad's framing, interject with a `🧭 بوصلة:` prefix and a **one-line** correction. Do not lecture. Wait for Ahmad's response before continuing. If he signals the drift is intentional (e.g., he is discussing a genuinely medical topic for a reason), silence the compass for the rest of the conversation.

| Pattern | What it means | Compass response |
|---|---|---|
| *"بروتوكول طبي"* / *"تشخيص"* | Medical drift | *"هذا دور المستشفى. العوق الاجتماعي المستهدف هنا؟"* |
| *"CBAHI"* / *"JCI"* / *"معايير صحية"* | Medical-accreditation mindset | *"MHRSD ≠ MOH. الإطار تأهيلي-اجتماعي"* |
| *"رعاية"* without *"تمكين"* | Welfare model | *"رعاية ≠ تمكين. ما يزيد القدرة لا يعتني فقط؟"* |
| *"المريض"* / *"المرضى"* | Hospital frame | *"المستفيد. الفرق جوهري"* |
| *"إعاقة"* without *"عوق اجتماعي"* | Medical-only | *"العنصر الطبي معطى. الاجتماعي — أين؟"* |
| *"ابتلاء"* / *"قدر"* as discussion-closure | Religious given as silent premise | *"الرحمة تعطي البصيرة للعمل، لا للقعود"* |
| *"ميؤوس منه"* / *"صعب"* | Low expectation | *"ميؤوس من ماذا تحديداً؟ حدّد البُعد المُهمَل"* |
| *"ثواب"* / *"أجر"* as primary driver | Charity dominance | *"العدالة، ليست الصدقة. الحقوق، ليست التطوّع"* |
| Focus on *"إنجازاتي"* without *"الأثر"* | Inward frame | *"الأثر على المستفيد. الإنجاز وعاء له"* |

**Application rules:**

1. One-line intervention — never a lecture.
2. Wait for Ahmad's response before continuing.
3. If the drift is intentional (a genuinely medical conversation), silence the compass for the remainder of that conversation.
4. Log recurrence. If the same drift repeats twice or more, note the pattern for memory review.

## Why this is load-bearing

The compass is not decorative. Ahmad's 2025 achievements (see [[ahmad-2025-achievements]]) all live in social-model territory, not clinical:

- Item #4 — *"Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities in KSA"* — empowerment ≠ treatment.
- Item #6 — *"تمكين"* — explicitly shifts the system from cost-based welfare to investment-in-capabilities.
- Item #8 — *"خارطة طريق لإعادة تأسيس منظومة حقوق الأشخاص ذوي الإعاقة"* — rights-based system, not a clinical protocol.

The vocabulary is diagnostic: تمكين، استثمار، حقوق، منظومة. These are social-reengineering words, not clinical ones.

The launchpad mistake that birthed this frame: earlier proposals framed [[basira]] and PT work in EHR / procedure / codebase terms — technically competent but categorically wrong. **Ahmad is not a PT practitioner with tech skills. He is a social-systems engineer in PT cover.** The compass enforces that distinction in every conversation.

## Connection to the ministerial ambition

Minister Ahmad Al-Rajhi's framing: *"نستهدف أن نكون الأول على العالم."* The compass explains *why* this is achievable — the dominant global framing treats disability as a medical condition first. If KSA leads by building a social-model digital ecosystem ([[basira]] + this compass + a dignity atlas + tamkeen policy-as-code), it IS first in the world. This is strategy, not marketing. The compass is the engine under the strategy.

## Where the compass is operationalised

- **Software surface:** [[basira-leadership-compass]] — the seven-tab بوصلة القيادة inside Basira turns the dial into a decision workspace for senior leadership.
- **Evidence stack:** [[basira]]'s seven-pillar evidence stack (see the project hub) — every pillar is a social-model validation (excellence, empowerment, rights, ranking), not a cure-or-diagnosis credential.
- **Vocabulary:** the rehabilitation/empowerment chain at MHRSD (Al-Ghamdi's shared services + Al-Qahtani's rehabilitation wakala) is the correct institutional home for social-model framing — the medical chain (MOH hospitals) is distinct and stays distinct.

## V4 operationalization (2026-06-04)

The compass dial is operationalised in the V4 rebuild ([[basira-v4-rebuild]]). Three points carry the frame into code:

- **The B1–B10 codes become a first-class field.** The ten dial types now have canonical codes (B1 عائق physical · B2 تحدٍّ procedural friction · B3 خصم declared adversary · B4 مضادّ systemic · B5 داعم مزيّف fake supporter · B6 مصدر خوف stigma source · B7 خوف internalised · B8 عُرف social norm · B9 ثقافة taboo · B10 مُسلَّم religious dogma), recovered verbatim from V2 `sql/022`. V4 carries `barrier_codes String[]` as a real column on empowerment/rights entities — not a SQL comment — so every capability states which barrier it dissolves. Detail in [[basira-v4-disability-empowerment-model]].
- **The medical-gateway barrier (البوابة الطبية) is the sharpest real-world instance.** The only door to ministry services is خدمة تقييم الإعاقة, which mandatorily requires أصل تقرير طبي → the system asks "ما تشخيصك؟" not "ما حلمك؟". V4 inverts it: the KNOW surface leads with goals + barriers + function; diagnosis is a mismatch-guard chip, never the header.
- **The frame is externally validated.** The ministry's own national report measures Outputs not Outcomes («وهم الإنجاز»), and the beneficiary rights charter affirms dignity + autonomy (`KB: harvest/14-f`) — so the social-handicap frame is the ministry's own diagnosis, not only Ahmad's. The cross-service care wires that dismantle barriers operationally live in [[basira-v4-cross-service-triggers]].

One honest tension to keep in view: the ministry's disability-classification table (جدول تصنيف الإعاقات, `KB: harvest/14-org`) is **severity-gated** for device/cash entitlement (مادة الصرف). V4 adopts its device-**appropriateness** rules (the anti-mismatch engine) but rejects مادة الصرف severity as a **service-access** gate — disability is never a ceiling.
