# MHRSD Era Timeline

Ahmad's employer ministry has been renamed twice during his tenure. Correct framing of any historical document or initiative requires knowing which era it belongs to.

## Era map

| Era | Ministry (Arabic) | Acronym | Domain (approx.) | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| pre-2016 | وزارة الشؤون الاجتماعية | **MOSA** | Social Affairs only | Pre-consolidation. Ahmad's ministry email appears to start here (~2014, per voice memory). |
| 2016 – 2020 | وزارة العمل والتنمية الاجتماعية | **MLSD** | Labor + Social Dev merger | Minister Majid Al-Qasabi era; operational improvement begins here. |
| 2020 – present | وزارة الموارد البشرية والتنمية الاجتماعية | **HRSD** / **MHRSD** | HR + Social Dev (current) | Rebrand under current minister Ahmad Al-Rajhi. |

## Majid Al-Qasabi inflection

Per Ahmad (2026-04-24 voice): before Minister Majid Al-Qasabi's MLSD-era engagement, the ministry was "dysfunctional — paying large sums of money but delivering little." Al-Qasabi began engaging younger staff directly; operational quality genuinely improved from that point.

**Framing rules for documentary evidence:**

- **Pre-Al-Qasabi (≤ ~2017)** — treat as legacy-bureaucracy context. Initiatives from this era often stalled.
- **Post-Al-Qasabi (MLSD era)** — reform narrative begins. Ahmad's involvement with formal assignments (see [[ahmad-formal-assignments]]) likely accelerates here.
- **Current HRSD (Ahmad Al-Rajhi)** — continuation of the reform trajectory; strategic modernisation (Vision 2030, digital transformation, HRSD Elite) intensifies.

## Retrieval rules

- When scanning PST / `sent.mbox` / Drive docs, tag by era; pre-2020 content uses MLSD terminology — do not treat as current HRSD.
- Majid Al-Qasabi is a recurring oral reference for Ahmad — treat as known marker, not a tangent to investigate each time.
- For any document whose language/branding precedes the current ministry, flag `valid_from:` in frontmatter to the era it represents, not the date Claude ingested it.
