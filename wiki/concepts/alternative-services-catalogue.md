# Alternative-Services Catalogue

## The delta this note carries

The vault already lists the alternative-service *mix* narratively (day-care, home-care, vocational, PWD homes — [[community-integration-project]]) and the **8 eligibility tracks + 9-value disqualification enum** as intake-routing gates ([[basira-v4-disability-empowerment-model]]). What is missing — and what Ahmad names as the **institutional-system gap** — is the catalogue *as a structured routing menu*: a first-class, eligibility-keyed list of what a beneficiary is a candidate FOR. **There is no catalogue today.** Basira can route a directive but has nothing to route TO.

## The catalogue as a menu (eligibility-keyed)

Two layers, each entry keyed by an eligibility rule (links into [[integration-readiness-classification]]):

- **Service tracks** — day-care, vocational rehab, social home-care, medical home-care, extended medical care, residential, orphan/elderly homes (the destination tracks; full track definitions in [[basira-v4-disability-empowerment-model]], not restated here).
- **Benefit / entitlement layer** — disability allowance (إعانة المعاقين), social security (الضمان الاجتماعي), assistive/compensatory devices, rental/transport assistance, MoH transfer.

The **legal hooks** for the benefit layer live in the Saudi Rights Law: health & medical rehabilitation **Art. 9** `[verified]`, social support → community integration **Art. 15** `[verified]`, assistive/compensatory devices **Art. 16** `[verified]`. Higher-numbered entitlements are reported but unconfirmed — customs exemption (reported Art. 17) `[verify]` ⚠️, banking/financing (reported Art. 18) `[verify]` ⚠️, developmental loans (reported Art. 19) `[verify]` ⚠️, housing (reported Art. 20) `[verify]` ⚠️ — present these as "reported as Art. X," never assert the numbers. Full provenance in [[saudi-disability-rights-framework]].

## The accountability inversion

The corrosive anti-pattern: **"service unavailable in region" is recorded as a reason the *person* can't leave** — a beneficiary property. It is a **system accountability gap to fix**, not a fact about the person. Region-availability is therefore a property of the *catalogue entry* (where the service exists, and where it is missing), surfaced as an accountability gap the system must close — the same honesty discipline as the cross-service interlocks ([[basira-v4-cross-service-triggers]]).

## Honest scope

The deck never structures these cleanly, and entries like a transport discount are absent in the source. Build the catalogue from what is verified; do **not** manufacture missing entries. The catalogue is also the structural answer to family-fear sub-fear #2 (financial) — see [[family-fear-barrier]].
