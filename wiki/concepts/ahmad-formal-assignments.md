# Ahmad's Formal Ministry Assignments

The credential title **أخصائي علاج طبيعي** (PT Specialist) describes Ahmad's licensure, not his scope. Across his ministry tenure (MOSA → MLSD → HRSD — see [[mhrsd-era-timeline]]), Ahmad has held six formal cross-cutting assignments in addition to his PT role. Any deliverable, nomination, or pitch that frames him as "just a PT specialist" is incomplete.
