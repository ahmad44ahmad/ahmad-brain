# MHRSD 2026 Health KPIs for Social Homes

## What this document is

A 43-indicator health-performance instrument issued for MHRSD **social-care homes** (الدور الاجتماعية: dar nursing for elderly + dar dependent populations) — distinct from the 2025 rehabilitation-centers scorecard ([[mhrsd-2025-kpi-master-scorecard]]).

Sheet title (verbatim): *مؤشرات الأداء الصحية للدور الاجتماعية 2026م*. Companion data-collection forms exist in the same Drive folder for two sub-classes: *دور الأحداث ودور الأيتام* (juvenile + orphan homes) and *مراكز التأهيل ودور المسنين* (rehab + elderly homes). This sheet is the *master indicator list*, not the data form.

## Structure

Each indicator carries **12 fields**:

1. *اسم المؤشر* — name
2. *وصف المؤشر* — description
3. *المعادلة* — formula
4. *الوحدة* — unit (نسبة / عدد / معدل)
5. *القطبية* — polarity (إيجابي / سلبي)
6. *المستهدف المعياري* — benchmark target (or لا يوجد / للقياس فقط)
7. *المستهدف الداخلي* — internal target
8. *النطاق الأصفر* — yellow band
9. *النطاق الأحمر* — red band
10. *التكرار* — frequency (mostly ربع سنوي, some نصف سنوي)
11. *التجميع* — aggregation (uniformly مرحلي = stage)
12. *مصدر البيانات* — data source

**No department weights.** The 2026 social-homes instrument is flat — every KPI is independently tracked, not summed into a center-aggregate. This is a structural difference from the 2025 rehab-centers scorecard.

Three PT-direct indicators in the 2026 social-homes set:

| KPI (AR) | English gloss | Polarity | Target | Data source |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| تيبس أو تصلب بالعضلات (contractures) | Muscle contractures | − | الانخفاض | سجلات العلاج الطبيعي |
| خدمات التأهيل الطبي والجبائر | Medical-rehab services + splints | + | 100% | سجلات العلاج الطبيعي |
| تحسن المهارات الحياتية والوظائف الحركية | Improvement in life skills + mobility | + | الارتفاع | سجلات العلاج الطبيعي والوظيفي |

**PT presence in 2026 vs 2025**: 2025 had 4 PT KPIs (6% scorecard weight) all *process* metrics (referral, splints, plan, sessions). 2026 social-homes adds *outcome* metrics — contractures, mobility improvement — which are clinically harder targets but more aligned with empowerment framing. **Trend toward outcome-tracking is documented in the 2026 instrument.**

## Critique inheritance + new flags

Of the three structural critiques carried from 2025 ([[mhrsd-2025-kpi-master-scorecard]]):

1. **Zero-event under-reporting persists** — pressure ulcers target 0%, severe fall injury 0-1%, restraint 0-3%. Same incentive shape.
2. **Medical-model framing partially relaxed** — communication-improvement, behavioral-improvement, mobility-improvement are *progress* metrics not *throughput* metrics. Closer to social-handicap-compass framing. But the bulk remains clinical-throughput.
3. **No beneficiary-self-report metric still** — patient-reported outcomes (pain, depression) are *recorded by staff*, not self-reported by beneficiaries. The dignity gap from [[social-handicap-compass]] is unaddressed.

New flag specific to 2026:

4. **"للقياس فقط" (measure-only) targets** — 4 indicators (overtime, antipsychotics use, anxiolytics use, nurse:beneficiary ratio) have no scoring threshold, only data collection. **This is honest signaling** that the ministry doesn't yet know what target to set; it's monitoring before deciding. Worth surfacing as a positive design choice.

## Operational read

For a center director: this instrument turns every clinical department's monthly records into a **dashboard with red/yellow/green bands**. Unlike the 2025 rehab-centers scorecard (additive weighted sum → director-level evaluation), the 2026 social-homes instrument is a **multi-axis dashboard** without a single aggregate. Each red band requires its own improvement plan (which itself is a KPI: "التحسين المستمر لأداء المؤشرات" — improvement plans for under-performing KPIs, target 100%).

**Improvement-plan KPI is recursive.** Every red band generates a required improvement plan, and the existence of those plans is itself measured.
