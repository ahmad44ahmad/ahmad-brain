# Al-Baha Branch Quality Unit 2025 Operational Plan

## What this is + the unit-of-analysis distinction

A regional-level operational plan for the **Quality + Performance Development Unit (وحدة الجودة وتطوير الأداء)** at the **Al-Baha HRSD branch** — i.e., the regional ministry office, **not** the rehabilitation center.

The branch supervises ~19 administrative departments (financial, HR, IT, social-supervision, family-protection, child-protection, vocational-empowerment, employment, mediation, oversight, etc.). The rehab center is **one** of those departments.

This means there are **two distinct quality functions**:

| Layer | Owner | Scope |
|---|---|---|
| Branch quality unit | Mariya Al-Mohammedi (under [[khalid-alzahrani]]) | All 19 branch departments + the rehab center |
| Center quality department | [[albaha-center-org]] — Ahmad Al-Shahri as رئيس قسم الجودة | Inside the rehab center only |

Ahmad's center-level Quality role and the branch-level Quality unit operate at **different layers**. Ahmad's work feeds *into* the branch quality unit's coverage of the rehab center.

## Unit identity

- **Title:** وحدة الجودة وتطوير الأداء — Quality and Performance Development Unit
- **Branch:** Al-Baha HRSD branch (regional office)
- **Year:** 2025 (1446 H)

**Team:**

- **Supervisor (إشراف):** **خالد بن صالح الزهراني** ([[khalid-alzahrani]]) — also Regional GM + assistant for development sector. Adds quality-unit supervision to his role profile.
- **Quality officer (مسؤولية):** **مارية بنت مقسم المقدمي** (Mariya bint Muqsim Al-Mohammedi)
  - Email: `M.Almohammedi@hrsd.gov.sa`
  - Phone: `0565113012`
  - **NEW PERSON in vault** — first appearance.

## Plan logic (3-step model)

1. **Translate strategy → projects.** Each branch department writes operational projects + initiatives.
2. **Measure performance periodically.** Cycle = monthly, last-week meeting.
3. **Distribute responsibility + continuous improvement.** Roles allocated across departments; non-performance escalated to branch director.

## Targets

- Raise efficiency across branch departments
- Achieve >90% completion on projects + initiatives
- Improve beneficiary-experience + quality projects

## Operational mechanism (4-stage)

1. Fill operational-project card (بطاقة المشروع التشغيلي)
2. Prepare follow-up + targets card (بطاقة المتابعة والمستهدفات)
3. Link projects to ministry's operational targets
4. Approve projects through the relevant administration

## What's noteworthy for the rehab center specifically

The rehab center's *branch-level* KPIs (per this plan) are **outcome-focused** in a way the center-internal scorecards are not:

- **Beneficiary satisfaction at 85%** — this is the same satisfaction sub-KPI that's the *only* self-report metric in the entire 2024–2025 KPI surface ([[mhrsd-2024-kpi-social-section]] domain 5.2). The branch quality unit cares about it. The medical-side scorecard ([[mhrsd-2025-kpi-master-scorecard]]) doesn't.
- **20 recreational activities** — overlaps with social-section domain 2 (programs + activities, 192/year center-internal). Branch tracks just the activity count, not the splits.

**Implication:** the branch-level lens is closer to *empowerment* framing than the center-internal medical-side scorecard. A Basira pitch that emphasises beneficiary-satisfaction + activity-program execution will resonate more with the branch quality unit + Khalid Al-Zahrani than with the medical departments.

## Strategic positioning of the unit

The plan explicitly anchors the unit in:

- *مستهدفات وزارة الموارد البشرية والتنمية الاجتماعية* — ministry-level targets
- *توجهات وكالة تمكين العملاء وخدمة العميل* — Customer Empowerment + Service Agency direction
- *تميز مؤسسي وجودة الأداء* — institutional excellence + performance quality

The customer-empowerment / service-experience anchor is **policy-aligned with [[basira]]**'s empowerment thesis. Branch quality is the natural intra-ministry alliance for any Basira pitch.

## Hot-line + escalation

The plan explicitly requests "قناة تواصل مباشرة (خط ساخن) مع وحدة الجودة" — a direct hotline between each department and the quality unit. Operational mechanism for raising obstacles + escalations.

For Ahmad: the hotline is via Mariya Al-Mohammedi (`M.Almohammedi@hrsd.gov.sa` / `0565113012`).

## Why this matters for [[basira]]

1. **Policy alignment.** The customer-empowerment / service-experience anchor is the same thesis Basira advances.
2. **Direct contact.** The named quality officer + hotline is a real intra-ministry channel.
3. **Branch-level outcomes ≠ center-level scorecards.** Pitching Basira at the branch quality layer (vs. medical-departments-and-scorecard layer) gets a more empowerment-friendly reception.
4. **Khalid Al-Zahrani.** Already in vault as *Step 0 for Basira escalation*. This plan confirms his quality-unit supervisor role on top of regional GM + assistant for development sector.
