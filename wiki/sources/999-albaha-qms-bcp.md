## Position in the stack — "Golden Thread"

The doc's own self-description for its framing:

> **"الخيط الذهبي ... البنية العصبية لنظام إدارة الجودة"**
> *The Golden Thread is the nervous system of the QMS.*

Three braids of the Golden Thread (الخيط الذهبي):
1. **Purpose** — Vision 2030 + empowerment of persons with disabilities.
2. **International compliance** — ISO 9001:2015 + CRPD.
3. **Excellence** — EFQM model.

The doc's claim is that these three normally run as silos ("quality committee vs safety committee vs compliance committee"); the **Integrated Management System (IMS — نظام الإدارة المتكامل)** fuses them into **"الجهد الواحد لنتائج متعددة"** ("one effort, multiple results"). CBAHI is not named anywhere; accreditation is referenced only generically as *معايير الاعتماد*.

## The Eight-Stage Satisfaction Measurement Cycle

The doc codifies an eight-stage cycle as the executive framework for satisfaction measurement. The source enumeration has an off-by-one rendering bug (item "5" labels both Stage 5 and Stage 6); the canonical list is:

| # | Arabic | English gist |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **تحديد أهداف القياس** | Define SMART measurement goals; example target: 80% overall satisfaction; approved + documented by center management. |
| 2 | **تحديد مجتمع وعينة الدراسة** | Define population + sample; **stratified random sampling** by age, gender, disability severity. |
| 3 | **تصميم وتطوير أدوات القياس** | Design instruments fitted to cognitive/communicative ability — visual pictorial, emoji, multimodal. |
| 4 | **التحقق من صدق وثبات أدوات القياس** | Validate instruments: expert-panel face validity, test-retest reliability, internal consistency; iterate. |
| 5 | **جمع البيانات (التنفيذ الميداني)** | Field collection; train the collection team; comfortable environment; **informed consent** (الموافقة المستنيرة) from beneficiary or guardian. |
| 6 | **تحليل البيانات وتفسيرها** | Quantitative % + qualitative themes; integrate into strengths/weaknesses. |
| 7 | **إعداد التقارير ونشر النتائج** | Audience-tailored: executive brief, department-detail, beneficiary/family pictorial bulletin; confidentiality preserved. |
| 8 | **الاستفادة من النتائج في التحسين المستمر** | Improvement teams, action plans, budget allocation, **Fast-Track** rapid-response for critical feedback, close-the-loop back to beneficiaries. |

**Four-phase rollout** (separate from the 8 stages): *Foundational (3–6 mo) → Pilot (n=15–20, 3 mo) → Expansion (6 mo) → Institutionalization.*

## Accessibility design for intellectual/cognitive disability

Surveys are redesigned under a person-centered (متمركز حول الشخص) methodology:

1. **Visual cues** — faces, emojis, thumbs-up/down replacing text scales.
2. **Minimum-text tablet UIs** — large icons, single-tap response (the *صوتي يُسمع* / *صوتك مسموع* app; note the two naming variants coexist in the source).
3. **Multimodal redundancy** — visual + kinesthetic + gamified + biometric, so any one channel being inaccessible doesn't exclude the beneficiary.
4. **Local-cultural anchoring** — Al-Baha heritage colours, foods, and dialect; القط العسيري motifs used as stimulus cues instead of abstract templates.
5. **Gamification** — *لعبة الوجوه* (Face Game), *لوحة النجوم* (Star Board), *صندوق السعادة* (Happiness Box), weekly inter-classroom competitions.
6. **Biometric inference** — *سوار المزاج الذكي* (Smart Mood Bracelet): HR, EDA, voice-tone as implicit satisfaction signal for non-verbal respondents.
7. **Proxy Reports** — used exceptionally, only where self-report is impossible; parents and caregivers are part of the sample, not substitutes.
8. **CRPD operationalised** — **"لا شيء عنا بدوننا"** ("Nothing about us without us") governs tool design.

## Governance — who owns what

- **إدارة المركز** — approves Stage-1 goals.
- **مكتب إدارة الجودة (QMO)** — owns the quality toolkit, NCR/CAPA ledger, RCA support.
- **رؤساء الأقسام** — conduct RCA with QMO support; own corrective actions.
- **فرق عمل التحسين** — small cross-disciplinary improvement teams formed post-measurement.
- **Expert panel (لجنة الخبراء)** — psychologists, SPED trainers, beneficiary-family representatives — Stage-4 face-validity.
- **IMS** — dissolves separate quality/safety/compliance committees into a unified structure.
- **MHRSD central** — *أكاديمية تجربة العميل* (Customer Experience Academy, Transformation Program product) supplies the ministry-level capability layer. See [[mhrsd-dev-sector-esystems]].
- **Partnerships** — *جمعية "سعي"* named for employment-integration; civil-society operationalises the care→empowerment pivot.

## Amplified-Risk Profile + "BCP"

The doc introduces **"نموذج المخاطر المضاعفة"** (Amplified Risk Profile) as a unique coinage: rehab centers are hybrid residential + clinical + vocational + recreational facilities, so standard OSH rules must be re-tuned because beneficiaries cannot self-rescue.

Risk classes covered (not presented as a numbered tier list in the source):
1. **Evacuation** — **PEEPs** (per-beneficiary plans) + horizontal evacuation + multi-sensory alarms. The 2–3 minute standard evacuation target is **explicitly rejected** as unrealistic for severe-mobility/cognitive-impairment cases.
2. **Ergonomics** — mechanical lifts + slide-sheets replacing manual lifting as the #1 occupational MSD risk.
3. **Infection control** — Wiqaya-aligned; environmental hygiene.
4. **Clinical emergencies** — seizure / behavioural crisis / vitals drop, routed through IoMT wearables.
5. **Staff-availability continuity** — documented case **RCA-2026-001** (safety-supervisor absence → field inspection halted → root cause = no trained backup → control = train a shift-supervisor substitute).
6. **Heat-stress** — staff-worn biometric sensors.
7. **Fall risk** — composite score from wearable + motion sensor + medication history.

Response posture: **Predictive** (AI) → **Preventive** (PEEPs, engineered handling) → **Reactive** (Fast-Track) → **Learning** (NCR→CAPA→PDCA).
