# MHRSD April 2025 Risk + Excellence + Innovation Bundle

## What this source is

A 30-file Drive folder titled `سياسات المخاطر الوزارة التميز الابتكار` — Gemini reviewed all available files. The folder is **MHRSD's April 2025 governance + risk + business-continuity policy promulgation**, issued by **الإدارة العامة للحوكمة والمخاطر والالتزام** (General Administration for Governance, Risk, and Compliance).

This is **official MHRSD policy material**, not Ahmad-authored. Two strategic value-propositions for the vault:

1. **Anchor [[ismail-alghamdi]]** with documentary evidence. The folder establishes Al-Ghamdi as **chair of the steering committee for business continuity + risk + governance** during the bundle's promulgation. CLAUDE.md and the people index have asserted Al-Ghamdi's strategic-patron role; this note is the source-anchor closing the audit's [[mhrsd-leadership]] gap.
2. **Document the regulatory surface Basira sits inside.** The cybersecurity + tech-change-management + accessibility + system-decommissioning policies in the bundle are the current ministry-side compliance framework Basira's governance layer must align with. The 11-document Basira compliance packet ([[basira]] §"Evidence stack" pillar 7, mapping to NCA ECC-2:2024 + CSCC-1:2019) operates **inside this regulatory surface**, not outside it.

## Layer 1 — Risk + business continuity + compliance (April 2025 core bundle)

Six policies promulgated 2025-04-21 → 2025-04-24, all approved by **the Steering Committee for Business Continuity, Risk and Governance chaired by Ismail bin Saeed Al-Ghamdi**:

| Policy | Version | Date | Stated objective |
|---|---|---|---|
| **سياسة إدارة المخاطر** (Risk Mgmt Policy, file 2) | **V4.0** | 2025-04-21 | Comprehensive standardised framework for identifying, analysing, evaluating, treating, and monitoring risks (strategic, operational, financial, compliance) — embedding risk-management into ministerial decision-making. |
| **إجراءات إدارة المخاطر** (Risk Mgmt Procedures, file 4) | **V3.0** | 2025-04-21 | Procedural standardisation — sequencing of identify → analyse → evaluate → response-planning → monitoring. |
| **سياسة الإبلاغ عن المخاطر** (Risk Reporting Policy, file 3) | **V2.0** | 2025-04-24 | Clear unified mechanism for senior-management + committee escalation of identified risks (realised or potential); ensures transparency and early problem-confrontation. |
| **سياسة إدارة الالتزام** (Compliance Mgmt Policy, file 14) | **V2.0** | 2025-04-21 | Ministry compliance with all legislative + legal + regulatory requirements + internal bylaws. Compliance team takes field-supervisory functions + corrective-plan development. |
| **سياسة إدارة استمرارية الأعمال** (Business Continuity Mgmt, file 13) | **V2.0** | 2025-04-21 | Continuity of essential ministry services + critical products under emergencies; includes **Business-Impact Analysis (BIA) + Disaster Recovery Plans (DRP)** to minimise impact, **especially in residential-care institutions (rehabilitation centers)**. Direct relevance to Al-Baha center continuity posture. |
| **إطار عمل إدارة الطوارئ** (Emergency Management Framework, file 12) | **V1.0** | 2025-04-22 | Procedural guidance for **Emergency Operations Manuals (EOM)** — facility + asset safe-and-effective operation under emergencies; preparedness, response, recovery requirements. |

**Cross-reference to Al-Baha posture.** Files [[al-baha-ic-archive]] (COVID-19 contingency layer) + [[999-institutional-excellence-innovation]] (5-star Red Crescent emergency-safety compliance) + this bundle's BIA/DRP/EOM framework form a **multi-source ladder** documenting that Al-Baha's emergency-safety posture isn't ad-hoc — it operates inside an actively-promulgated ministerial framework Basira can plausibly anchor against.

## Layer 2 — Innovation + institutional excellence + digital governance

Eight policies / initiatives, mostly issued by **وكالة التحول الرقمي** (Digital Transformation Wakala) and **الإدارة العامة للتميز المؤسسي** (Institutional Excellence GA):

| Initiative | Owner | Summary |
|---|---|---|
| **الحاضنة الافتراضية للأفكار الإبداعية** (Virtual Idea Incubator, file 7) | Institutional Excellence GA | Sustainable platform for receiving creative ideas from ministry staff. Cites **ISO 56002** (innovation management standard). Ideas matched to criteria → converted to projects with added-value. |
| **آلية توثيق المشاريع والمبادرات** (Project Documentation Mechanism, file 5) | وكالة الشؤون الاستراتيجية / PMO | 6-step methodology for documenting Vision-initiative outputs + KPIs on the approved system. Quality-of-documentation standards for transparency + accountability. |
| **جائزة التميز المؤسسي** (Institutional Excellence Award, files 18, 22) | Institutional Excellence GA | Service enabling ministry staff to nominate + participate. **Service link surfaced on خدماتي platform with Ahmad's account address: `shahri015@hrsd.gov.sa`** — operational-trace evidence Ahmad's account had access. |
| **سياسة إدارة التغيير التقني** (Tech Change Mgmt Policy, files 6, 9) | الإدارة العامة للحوكمة الرقمية / Digital Wakala | Manages tech changes (systems, policies, procedures); change-request procedures (normal + emergency). V2.0 dated 2024-11-20. |
| **سياسة سهولة الوصول** (Accessibility Policy, file 19) | Digital Governance GA | Ensures PWD accessibility to ministry digital services. **Aligns with WCAG.** Direct Vision-2030 empowerment alignment. |
| **سياسة إخراج الأنظمة من الخدمة** (System Decommissioning, file 15) | Digital Governance GA | Orderly discontinuation of legacy systems + services; documentation of reasons + steps; ensures continuity + data security. |
| **سياسة الاستخدام المقبول للأصول المعلوماتية** (Acceptable Use of IT Assets, file 8) | الإدارة العامة للأمن السيبراني / Digital Wakala | Rules + responsibilities for ministry asset use. **V9.0 dated 2025-01-01, signed by Minister Ahmed Al-Rajhi personally** — high signature-weight item. |
| **سياسات وعمليات إدارة الحوادث** (IT Incident Mgmt, file 11) | IT Operations GA / Digital Wakala | Standardised IT-incident handling; rapid service restoration; minimised business impact. V1.2 dated 2020-09-10 (oldest in bundle — predates the 2025 reorganisation). |

**Why this matters for [[basira]].** Six of the eight innovation-layer policies fall under وكالة التحول الرقمي's portfolio. Basira lives inside that wakala's regulatory surface for change-management, accessibility, and acceptable-use compliance. The 11-document compliance packet at `C:\dev\basira\docs\` should cross-reference these specific ministerial policies to demonstrate **compatibility-by-design** rather than retroactive alignment.
