## Why this folder is priority-3

This folder is Ahmad's intellectual signature — the entire body of work treating Saudi disability services as a systems-transformation problem (medical/care model → social/rights model per CRPD). It anchors:

- Basira's conceptual legitimacy ("not a tech project — the digital layer of a validated-excellence center scaling to 36 centers").
- The Kingdom-level PT supervisor nomination's differentiating narrative (Ahmad as engineer + clinician, not only clinician).
- Any proposal addressed to senior ministry leadership (Al-Ghamdi, the Minister, wakala offices) — these docs provide the framing patrons expect.

Without this folder, Claude can retrieve Ahmad's facts but not Ahmad's voice.

## Core theme

**Strategic + methodological transformation of disability services in Saudi Arabia** — from a traditional medical/care model toward a social/rights model centered on empowerment. Rooted in:

- **CRPD** — UN Convention on the Rights of Persons with Disabilities (rights-based frame).
- **Vision 2030** — alignment with national transformation targets.
- **ISO 9001** / **EFQM** / **TQM** — institutional-excellence frameworks used to operationalise the transformation.
