# Training Development Project

## What this source is

A 32+ file Drive folder titled `مشروع تطوير التدريب` documenting the **operational training architecture** for Phase-1 of [[community-integration-project]]. The two notes are **tightly coupled** — community-integration is the strategic vision; this is the training preparation that makes Phase-1 executable.

Vault-significance: documents the **Individualised Educational Program (IEP) methodology** the ministry uses + the specific decree forming the **«طي القيد» steering committee** chaired by the **deputy minister for social development** (the Wakil for التأهيل والتوجيه الاجتماعي — currently [[nasser-alqahtani]], previously al-Wuhaibi). High-leverage cross-anchor for [[mhrsd-leadership]].

## Governance structure

| Instrument | Reference | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| **Circular #116794** (files 3, 4, 25, 26) | -- | Activates training + family-guidance programs for Phase-1 community-integration targets. |
| **Targeting criteria** (files 25, 26) | -- | Targets *«حالات مخالفة للنظام»* (cases not meeting residency criteria per the basic disability-services bylaw) attached to comprehensive rehab centers. Destinations: day-care, home-care, vocational rehab. |
| **Procedural commitment** (files 25, 26) | -- | Centers must intensify training + family-guidance for these cases within **3 months max** per case. Apply approved templates from *دليل توحيد إجراءات العمل* (Social + Health departments). Includes mandatory family-context study. |
| **«طي القيد» Steering Committee** (files 27, 28) | Decree forming the committee | **Chaired by the deputy minister for social development** (= the Wakil for Rehabilitation + Social Guidance). Members: ministry deputies (وكلاء الوزارة). Mission: ensure service-coordination + apply طي القيد procedures via regulatory channels within project timeline. |

**The «طي القيد» framing.** This phrase literally means *"folding / removing the residency record"* — administrative process for transitioning a case OUT of the residency-records system, the documentary mirror of the Phase-1 community integration. Worth recording as procedural-vocabulary. The steering committee named here is only the *approval* gate: the full closure path is a **two-committee verify→approve sequence** (Technical verifies the تعميم-117792 dossier → Steering approves) now detailed in [[tai-alqaid-closure-gate]].

## IEP methodology (Individualised Educational Program)

The folder treats IEP as a **written rights instrument** for the beneficiary + family. File 8 carries the methodology spec:

**Definition**: a written statement for each special-needs beneficiary, drafted in a qualified training-team meeting, formulated to match individual needs.

**Importance** (3-pillar):
1. Preserves beneficiary + family rights.
2. Enables planned, organised training.
3. Prefers least-restrictive environment for the beneficiary.

**Preparation stages**:
1. Determine current performance level (behavioural observation + measurement scales).
2. Analyse data → graph representation.
3. Comprehensive report.
4. Program design (long-term + short-term goals).

**Program elements**:
1. **General objectives** (long-term / annual) — formulated with non-measurable verbs (يتقن، يطور، ينمي).
2. **Specific objectives** (short-term) — formulated with measurable verbs.
3. Time periods.
4. Support services.

## HELP & BOB methodology

Files 16, 7 reference the **HELP & BOB methodology (4th edition, ages 1-18 years)** for autism spectrum disorder + developmental disorders evaluation + programming. Concrete IEP templates (Vocational Rehab Plan: Chef; HELP plan ages 15-18) demonstrate operational application. File 15 carries the **Initial Behavioural Observation Form**:

- **Behavioural problems**: harming others, self-harm, hyperactivity.
- **Independence skills**: requesting toilet, eating independently.
- **Social skills**: greeting, social-play participation.

**Vault note**: HELP & BOB is the operational-tooling layer underneath the *«مؤشر الاستقلالية»* (Autonomy Score) proposed in [[999-zero-paper-master]]. Where the Compass (بوصلة) at the Basira-product layer reformulates the *measurement* axis, HELP & BOB is the *evaluation* methodology data-source the Compass would draw from.

## Training-plan archetypes (templates surfaced)

| Plan type | Domain | Sample objectives |
|---|---|---|
| **Vocational Rehab — Chef** (file 7) | Professional + social + academic | Know cooking terminology · master kitchen-safety rules · distinguish meat-cutting board vs vegetable-cutting board (quality criteria) · respect bathing etiquette · use appropriate social phrases |
| **IEP — General** | Cognitive + linguistic + sensory + social | Sequence pictorial story · complete story-events from picture · describe image with 3-word sentence · sequence body-movements (toothbrushing) · know names of surroundings + avoid risks · accept commands |
| **Independence + Social** | Self-care + acceptable behaviours | Operate mechanical toy unaided · distinguish men's vs women's restrooms in new locations · choose clothing appropriate to context (mosque / work / home) · share + initiate cooperation |

These templates are **operational vocabulary** for what "rehabilitation success" means at the per-beneficiary level — much more concrete than the strategic-vocabulary of [[empowerment-thesis-corpus]] or [[999-zero-paper-master]].

## Honest flagging

1. **«حالات مخالفة للنظام» language.** Same caveat as [[community-integration-project]] — the regulatory category names cases that don't meet residency criteria; these are people. Treat the framing as administrative, not moral, when surfacing externally.
2. **3-month integration window** is a stated requirement, not measured outcome. Whether centers actually achieve it for most cases is unknown from the folder.
3. **HELP & BOB 4th-edition currency.** The methodology version is dated; verify still-current before citing as live ministry reference.
4. **No completion-date stamps on most templates.** Treat as catalog of methodology, not chronology.
