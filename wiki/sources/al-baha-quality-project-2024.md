# Al-Baha Quality Project 2024 — Evidence Stack

## What this folder is

A 110+ file Drive folder titled *مشروع ما تم في المركز من قبل الجودة من عام 2024 الي مشاكل الادارة و طلب الاعفاء* (loosely: "What the Quality Department accomplished at the center from 2024, through to administrative problems and the resignation request"). JSONL idx #2 and idx #46 are two Gemini passes over the same folder; the audit flagged the duplicate at §2.3.

The folder is **not an organic archive**. It is a **self-compiled evidence dossier** — Ahmad assembled it as a single coherent case-file, with the chronology baked into the folder name itself. Every layer documents the previous layer failing:

> Quality work → operational escalations → agency responses → administrative friction → grievance/escalation memos → resignation request.

That structure makes it the canonical proof-of-implementation corpus for Ahmad's Quality work at the Al-Baha Comprehensive Rehabilitation Center, and simultaneously the upstream source for the forthcoming F-4 grievance-case synthesis.

## Why this note exists

[[999-albaha-qms-bcp]] is a *methodology synthesis* — it describes the QMS framework. **This folder is the evidence corpus underneath that synthesis.** Without this distillation, the Basira evidence stack rests on a Gemini-rephrasing of an LLM-synthesised Gdoc; with this distillation, the stack reaches into the operational artefacts Ahmad himself produced.

Specifically this note anchors:

- The "الخيط الذهبي" (Golden Thread) architecture asserted in `999-albaha-qms-bcp` (compliance ISO → excellence EFQM → purpose empowerment).
- The ISO 9001:2015 implementation claim in [[ahmad-2025-achievements]].
- The Quality-head formal assignment in [[ahmad-formal-assignments]].
- Ahmad's role-evidence in [[albaha-center-org]] (he heads قسم الجودة).
- The forthcoming overtime-grievance synthesis (F-4) — this folder is one upstream source, treated with light touch here.

## Chronological narrative — what the Quality Department actually did

Inferred arc from the JSONL passes (mid-2024 → early 2025 / Hijri 1446):

1. **QMS design and rollout (2024 H1–H2).** Department drafts an integrated QMS for the center anchored on three pillars: ISO 9001:2015 (compliance), EFQM (excellence — ministry-approved), and *الاستراتيجية الوطنية لتمكين ذوي الإعاقة* (purpose). Core deliverables: *دليل نظام إدارة الجودة المتكامل* (Integrated QMS Handbook) and *بناء إطار حوكمة الجودة لمركز التأهيل* (Quality Governance Framework).
2. **Process inventory per ISO 9001 clause 4.4.** Cross-departmental process mapping. The secretariat unit (وحدة السكرتارية, head: أحمد الحوالي — distinct from Ahmad Al-Shahri) is the worked example; medical/social inventories presumably exist in the un-sampled 50 files.
3. **Balanced Scorecard.** *الجزء الخامس — لوحة مؤشرات الأداء الرئيسية المتوازنة* lays out the four BSC dimensions: beneficiaries, internal processes, learning & growth, financial.
4. **Qualitative-indicator tracking.** *جدول متابعة المؤشرات النوعية لقطاع التنمية الاجتماعية* (file 24) — to become active 2025-06-01, ties center-level reporting into the social-development sector's qualitative scorecard.
5. **Operational-risk escalation cycle (late 2024).** Quality Department compiles a "challenges table" — CCTV, fire-pump linkage, lift/safety maintenance — and forwards to *الوكالة المساعدة للشؤون الفنية* (Assistant Agency for Technical Affairs). Agency responses are folded back into the table; QA-side closure of observations follows once agency visibility is documented.
6. **Diwan General Accounting observations closed (end-2024).** File 35.
7. **Admin-correction committee ordered.** قرار إداري 26873 (1446/06/24H) forms a committee to draft corrective plans for *إدارة التأهيل الاجتماعي* observations within 10 days (files 28, 42).
8. **Agency-escalation breakdown after end-2024 agencies meeting** (file 5). Folder records *"هروب بعض الوكالات من التصعيد"* — agencies retreating from escalation; HR-system case evidence requested but not provided; staffing gaps deflected to operating-contract scope.
9. **Grievance/escalation arc (Ahmad personally).** Working-hours dispute, 2021 8-hour circular reapplied in 2024, *"قرار الإيقاف عن العمل التعسفي"* (arbitrary work-suspension decision), urgent escalation memo (files 11, 13, 26).
10. **Resignation/exemption request (طلب الإعفاء).** The folder's terminal event. **Not distilled here** — see §"Sensitive zone" below.

## The Golden Thread — load-bearing architectural quote

The folder makes the framing explicit (preserved verbatim):

> النظام يربط **الغاية** (التمكين والدمج وفقاً للاستراتيجية الوطنية لذوي الإعاقة) — **التميز** (EFQM المعتمد من الوزارة) — **الامتثال** (ISO 9001:2015)، ويُعتبر بمثابة "نظام التشغيل" الاستراتيجي للمركز.

That phrasing — *"نظام التشغيل" الاستراتيجي للمركز* — is the same "Center Operating System (cOS)" claim that appears in [[999-albaha-qms-bcp]]. The methodology synthesis describes the cOS; this folder is its evidence trail.

- *بناء إطار حوكمة الجودة لمركز التأهيل* — governance framework doc.
- ISO 9001 clause 4.4 process workbook → operationalises compliance.
- Balanced Scorecard (file 36) → operationalises excellence.
- Qualitative-indicator tracker (file 24) → operationalises purpose.

**Regional level (Al-Baha branch).**

- *إدارة المشروعات* and *إدارة المالية* — notified on the CCTV escalation.
- *إدارة العمليات بفرع الوزارة* — fire-safety sprinkler installation.
- *إدارة التأهيل الاجتماعي* — origin of observations that triggered Decision 26873.
- *لجنة الخطط التصحيحية* — ad-hoc committee formed by Decision 26873.

**Ministry level (HRSD HQ).**

- *الوكالة المساعدة للشؤون الفنية* (Assistant Agency for Technical Affairs) — primary escalation counterparty for CCTV / lifts / fire pumps.
- "الوكالات العليا" referenced throughout — escalations reach multiple وكالات at HQ level. See [[mhrsd-leadership]] for the routing map.

**Judicial.**

- *محكمة الاستئناف الإدارية بمنطقة عسير* — Administrative Court of Appeal, Asir region. Jurisdiction over HRSD Al-Baha branch appeals.

**Escalation pattern.** Quality Department → regional admin → ministry agencies → (when unresolved) → administrative court / grievance / resignation request. The folder documents every layer failing in turn. This is the structural finding the wiki note should preserve — the folder is not just evidence of *good* QMS work; it is evidence of *institutional non-response* to good QMS work.
