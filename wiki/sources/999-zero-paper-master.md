# 999 — Zero-Paper Master

## What this document is

A Gemini/NotebookLM thematic-Q&A synthesis stored as a Google Doc with the verbose Drive title *«999 تنسيق Markdown موضوع شامل عن عمليات المركز و حوكمتها و الجودة و الرقمنة و تطبيق صفر ورق»*. ~547,000 chars / ~961 kB exported plain text, 5,488 lines, ~70 sections. **Not Ahmad-authored** — every section opens with the AI-assistant persona *«بصفتي خبيراً ميدانياً ومخططاً استراتيجياً…»* and refers to underlying sources in the third person (*«المصادر»*). Same provenance class as [[999-institutional-excellence-innovation]] (same Drive owner workspace, same modifiedTime to the second — they look like sibling outputs of one authoring session).

The doc is **two passes of the same thematic series concatenated**. Pass 1 (lines 20–4286, ~274k chars, 50 sections) has well-spaced freestanding sections; pass 2 (lines 4288–5505, ~273k chars, ~22 sections) re-executes most of pass 1 with `## ` markdown heading prefixes inline. ## Basira product anatomy (the canonical 5-component statement)

The doc presents Basira as a *«بنية عصبية رقمية»* (digital nervous-system) of five interconnected components, not as an EHR or archive layer:

1. **«السجل الذهبي»** (Master Record) — single source of truth for beneficiary identity (*«مصدراً واحداً للحقيقة»*). Distinct from the *الخيط الذهبي* QMS metaphor and from F-5.1's *الدليل الذهبي للتميز المؤسسي* — different metaphors despite shared *الذهبي* root.
2. **Rehab + Empowerment Module** — individualised care + empowerment-pathway tracking; the productisation of *من الرعاية إلى التمكين*.
3. **Clinical Module** — clinical operations, medication management, incident reporting (OVR linkage).
4. **«ملف الكرامة»** (Dignity File) — the humanisation-layer signature; documents preferred-name, sensory tolerances, *«سجل الحسنات»* (Merit Ledger) for daily small achievements, personality type, dignity-preserving care notes.
5. **Logistics Module** — facilities, supplies, maintenance, evacuation logistics.

Three strategic goals span all five components:

- **«أنسنة الرعاية»** — humanisation of care (technical-services humanisation register).
- **«صفرية الورق» (Zero Paper)** — paperlessness as a system property; the doc canonises *صفرية الورق* (12 hits) rather than the MHRSD-registered initiative-form *صفر ورق* used in [[basira]] and [[ahmad-2025-achievements]]. Both forms are valid in their respective registers.
- **«الحوكمة الاستباقية»** — proactive governance; risk-management-by-prediction rather than reaction.

> *«صفرية الورق (Zero Paper): الانتقال من أكوام الملفات المعرضة للضياع إلى رقابة رقمية كاملة ولحظية على العمليات اليومية (مناوبات، تغذية، صيانة).»* — line 362; the canonical Zero-Paper definition. Reiterated verbatim at lines 4302 and 5488.

## ملف الكرامة (Dignity File) — the humanisation-layer signature

The single most distinctive product-design coinage in the doc. Appears 28× across 4 dedicated sections (lines 296, 348, 4481, plus inline at every Basira-recap). Frames Basira's dignity-preservation layer as a documented file rather than an algorithmic alert.

> *«إن "ملف الكرامة" هو ما ينقل مركز التأهيل من مجرد "مستودع بشري" يحفظ الأجساد، إلى "بيئة حاضنة" تحفظ الأرواح والكرامة.»* — line 4485; the dignity-file thesis statement.

Sub-feature *«سجل الحسنات»* (Merit Ledger / Good-Deeds Registry, 12 hits) logs daily small achievements as positive feedback for caregivers and family. The Saudi-Islamic register (*حسنات*) is deliberate and indigenous — pre-empts the Western policy-import critique applied to Pay-for-Success and similar imports.

**Open question (flagged for Ahmad).** F-5.1 canonises *«خوارزمية الإحسان»* as Basira's beneficiary-preference humanisation feature; F-5.3 canonises *«ملف الكرامة»* for what looks like the same surface. Two possibilities: (a) they are competing names for the same feature; (b) ملف الكرامة is the data store and خوارزمية الإحسان is the alerting algorithm reading from it. Vault should not silently pick one. See [[empowerment-vocabulary]] §Basira-product for the conflict pair.

## بوصلة (Compass) — 4-dimension success criteria

A standalone section (lines 720–764) proposing a wholesale replacement of the legacy "service-count" KPI with four new dimensions:

1. *«مؤشر الاستقلالية»* (Autonomy Score) — degree of self-determination achieved.
2. *«المساهمة النشطة»* (Active Contribution) — beneficiary's economic / social contribution.
3. *«الأثر الإنساني»* (Human Impact) — quality-of-life delta over baseline.
4. *«الاستدامة»* (Sustainability) — durability of gains over time.

> *«لقد حدث انقلاب كامل في مفهوم النجاح. نحن ننتقل من "مقياس الخدمات" إلى "مقياس الأثر الإنساني".»* — line 724; the Compass-section opening. Complements *من طغيان الكم إلى حوكمة الأثر* from the master portfolio and inverts the *طغيان المؤشرات الكمية* / *صفر حوادث* anti-pattern from [[empowerment-vocabulary]] §Critique.

Live status unclear — the doc reads as if the Compass is deployed inside Basira, but no other vault note confirms this. Treat as **doctrinal proposal until verified**.

## مساعد الظل (Shadow Assistant / Development Coach)

A dedicated employment-empowerment mechanism (lines 814–858 + 4579–4612, 22 hits total). Frames a per-employee mediator role for PWD workers — not a personal aide but a *«مهندس علاقات مهنية»* (professional-relationships engineer) removing invisible workplace barriers.

> *«التحدي الحقيقي ليس توظيف الشخص ذي الإعاقة، بل الاستدامة والتكيف. هنا يأتي دور "مساعد الظل" ليس كمرافق شخصي دائم، بل كـ "مهندس علاقات مهنية" يضمن نجاح الدمج.»* — line 818.

Deployment ratio cited: 1 coach per 13 employees (line 4581). **Open question (flagged for Ahmad)**: is مساعد الظل (a) a Basira product surface, (b) an MHRSD national-policy proposal, or (c) an Al-Baha-internal pilot? Line 814 frames it as a "national strategy mechanism" but line 4581 reads as a deployable model. Status unclear.

The doc also names *«فخ الإعانة»* (Disability-Allowance Trap) — the structural employment-disincentive: PWD workers fear losing the 4,000 SAR monthly stipend if formally employed. Argument-shape vocabulary in the same register as *هدر رأس المال البشري* from [[empowerment-vocabulary]] §Critique.

## Engineering-as-dignity register

A cluster of design-principle phrases that re-cast occupational engineering as a dignity discipline:

- **«هندسة الرفع»** (Lifting-Engineering, 12 hits) — the strategic shift from "training staff in proper lifting form" to "engineering the lift mechanically out of the staff's hands." Direct OSH design-principle. Pairs with the *نموذج السلامة ثنائي العميل* (Dual-Client Safety Model, 18 hits — variant inflection of F-5.1's *السلامة ثنائية العميل*).
- **«هندسة الكرامة»** (Dignity-Engineering) — universal-design framed as engineering rather than accessibility-compliance.

> *«لا يمكن فصل سلامة الموظف عن سلامة النزيل. نحن نتبنى "نموذج السلامة ثنائي العميل". إذا أُصيب الموظف في ظهره، فلن يستطيع نقل النزيل بأمان، وقد يسقط النزيل معه.»* — line 60; concrete cause-chain reasoning for dual-client safety.

Local-color product-design touches: *«غيمة الباحة»* + *«عَطَف»* (cartoon characters used in an IDD satisfaction-survey app), *«سوار المزاج الذكي»* (Smart Mood Bracelet — proposed 3-button wireless wearable for IDD beneficiaries to flag mood state), *«مكاني بينكم»* (anti-stigma awareness campaign — possibly an external MHRSD campaign, verify).

## Honest flagging

- **Aspirational-as-actual risk.** The doc describes Basira features (*سجل الحسنات*, predictive-health AI, camera-feed integration, Compass KPIs) as if they exist in the deployed system. Whether `C:\dev\basira` v2 has all of these implemented is a separate question — the doc gives no implementation status. **Quote any Basira-feature claim from this doc with "as designed" or "as described", not "as deployed".**
- **Gemini synthesis, not Ahmad authorship.** Treat all claims as *narrated*, not *primary*. Where this doc disagrees with [[basira]] or [[999-institutional-excellence-innovation]], the Ahmad-validated notes win.
- **Heavy internal duplication** (pass-1 / pass-2 reruns, ~50% of the file). Citation by line number alone is unstable across the duplicate sections — when citing, prefer the pass-1 line (lower number) and note when content also appears in pass-2.
- **No external-source attribution survives the export.** Some footnote-style citation markers exist as square " " markers but no specific source attributions; same Gemini-citation-marker problem as F-5.1.
- **No sensitive content detected.** No litigation, no third-party PII, no credentials.
