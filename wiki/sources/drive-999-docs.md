## Pattern

- Most 999 docs are research-style comprehensive treatments (30k – 1MB each) on single-topic deep-dives.
- Topic clusters: **rehabilitation centers** · **disability system reform** · **quality + governance** · **zero-paper / digitization** · **institutional excellence**.
- Some exist both as Gdoc and PDF — the PDFs are typically final exports.
- The "500 صفحة" marker confirms intentional long-form.

## Extraction priority (when Ahmad triggers a topic)

1. **Highest** — `999 مركز التأهيل الشامل موضوع شامل 500 صفحة` — the master document. Extract: section hierarchy, key claims, recurring frameworks, numbered recommendations.
2. **High** — `999 منظومة رعاية وتمكين الأشخاص ذوي الإعاقة` — national-initiative proposal. Maps to [[ahmad-formal-assignments]] row 5 (Disability) + row 6 (Non-gov rehab committee).
3. **High** — `999 نظام إدارة جودة العمليات والامتثال …` — maps to [[ahmad-formal-assignments]] row 1 (Quality).
4. **Medium** — `999 تحقيق التميز المؤسسي …` — institutional-excellence context.
5. **Lower** — duplicate/alternate versions.

## Purpose

Ahmad's operating pattern: 999-prefixed docs are "long-form thinking containers" — deep comprehensive treatments, not chat-style. They are **not drafts of deliverables** — they are the thinking substrate from which deliverables are later extracted. Treat as source material, not output.
