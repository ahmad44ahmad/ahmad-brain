## BICSL-RC — the trainer framework

**Basic Infection-Control Skills License — Rehabilitation Centers** is the strategic initiative whose certified trainers Ahmad is one of. Beyond clinical content, BICSL-RC carries an **explicit framing pivot**:

> *تحول من نموذج الوقاية "الطبي" البحت إلى نموذج "اجتماعي حياتي" شامل يتناسب مع طبيعة الرعاية السكنية طويلة الأمد*
> ("shift from a purely *medical* prevention model to a comprehensive *social-life* model matching the nature of long-term residential care")

This is **the same medical-vs-social pivot** that anchors [[empowerment-thesis-corpus]] and [[empowerment-vocabulary]]. The BICSL-RC framework is one of the few places where the empowerment-thesis pivot is operationalised at the protocol level — IC discipline reframed as a social discipline, not a clinical-only one. Worth noting that the pivot pre-exists Basira's pitch arc; Ahmad has been operating inside this conceptual framework for years.

**Curriculum components** (from the engineering-substrate-folder cross-reference + this folder's BICSL-RC manual):

- 5 hand-hygiene moments (اللحظات الخمس)
- PPE selection + correct use
- Sharps / needle-stick exposure handling
- Transmission-based isolation precautions (contact / droplet / airborne)
- N95 fit testing + PAPR (Powered Air-Purifying Respirator) as fit-test fallback
- Medical-waste handling
- Rehab-center-tailored procedures

Ahmad's operational contribution documented in `خطة تقييم مكافحة العدوى المعالجة.pptx`:

> *"وجود برنامج تثقيفي و تدريب مستمر و (موثق) مع المصدر العلمي الموثق للسياسات"*

— a documented continuous-training program with citation-anchored policy sources. **Completion date: 2024-07-21.** This is the kind of artefact that operationalises the Edition-2 policy P17 ("Basic IC training policy") at center level. Cross-link: this connects to [[ahmad-2025-achievements]] item #5 — *دليل إرشادي شامل لمكافحة العدوى لإدارة سلامة المستفيد* (comprehensive IC training manual, 2025-02-12 → 2025-05-14).
