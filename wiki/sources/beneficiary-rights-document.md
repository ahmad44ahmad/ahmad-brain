# Beneficiary Rights Document

## What this source is

A 6-file Drive folder titled `وثيقة حقوق المستفيدين` documenting MHRSD's **beneficiary-rights + anti-abuse infrastructure** at residential rehabilitation centers. Two ministerial circulars + workshop-training materials on abuse-type taxonomy.

This is **official MHRSD policy material**, not Ahmad-authored. Vault-significance: documents the **rights-and-safeguarding protocol** that the center operates under, plus carries a useful historical anchor in the Wakil signature on circular #228597.

## Layer 1 — Rights + duties documents (Circular #35746)

**Circular #35746, dated 4/2/1441 H (≈ 2019-09-29 G)** — issued to ministry regional-branch directors, mandating that rights + duties documents be **printed in A3 format and posted prominently** in administrative halls + visit areas.

### Beneficiary Rights Document

The document guarantees five core rights for residents at comprehensive rehabilitation centers (verbatim list):

1. Complete explanation of regulations + procedures.
2. Comprehensive social care + appropriate rehabilitation services + family participation in treatment-plan development.
3. Healthy environment that promotes health, well-being, self-respect, and **autonomy (الاستقلال الذاتي)**.
4. Security and **social protection** + prevention of physical, verbal, and psychological abuse.
5. Visits from all family members + periodic communication facilities.

The autonomy + family-participation + abuse-prevention triad in this 2019 document **pre-figures the empowerment-pivot vocabulary** of [[empowerment-vocabulary]] §Vision (especially *مؤشر الاستقلالية* later operationalised in [[999-zero-paper-master]]) — the conceptual seeds were already in ministerial circular form 5+ years before the Basira / Zero-Paper synthesis.

### Family Duties Document

The reciprocal document obligates families to:

1. Provide complete + accurate medical and social information about the beneficiary.
2. Follow center instructions and guidelines.
3. Report changes (address, contact, travel).
4. **Respect center staff + refrain from physical or verbal aggression.**
5. Visit + communicate periodically + show love and care.
6. Preserve beneficiary's financial rights + maintain medical appointments.

## Layer 2 — Abuse + Neglect Handling Protocol (Circular #228597)

**Circular #228597, dated 26/8/1441 H (≈ 2020-04-19 G)** — signed by **Dr. Abdullah bin Ahmed Al-Wuhaibi**, then **Wakil for Rehabilitation + Social Guidance**. Establishes the abuse + neglect reporting framework coordinated with **Social Protection Unit + Reports Centre 1919**.

**Historical anchor**: Al-Wuhaibi's signature on this circular dates his Wakala tenure to at least 2020-04. [[mhrsd-leadership]] now lists [[nasser-alqahtani]] as the current وكيل التأهيل والتوجيه الاجتماعي (replaced Al-Wuhaibi). This circular is one of the cleanest documentary anchors for the ministerial-leadership-transition timeline.

### Mandatory procedures by abuse/neglect type

| Type | Procedure |
|---|---|
| **Family neglect (visit absence)** | (1) Social-worker fills contact form, attempts contact 4× per month at varied times. (2) On unsatisfactory response, fills **confidential reporting form**, escalates via official email `1919@mlsd.gov.sa`. (3) Neglect >1 year: escalate to **regional governor** (الحاكم الإداري للمنطقة). |
| **Physical / psychological / verbal abuse** (from family or staff) | (1) Direct case-worker (social worker, social monitor, psychologist, or center director) fills **immediate injury form**. (2) Confidential reporting form via official email to 1919, attaching: social-research form + psychological-assessment form + medical report (if physical) + evidence photos. |
| **Document or health neglect** (e.g. failure to obtain national ID, failure to complete vaccinations) | Classified as neglect under **child-protection law + anti-abuse law**; requires intervention procedures to protect the child / beneficiary. |

## Layer 3 — Six abuse-type taxonomy

Workshop file (file 264) defines abuse types with specific regulatory definitions:

| Type | Definition (summarised) |
|---|---|
| **Physical** | Causing physical harm via hand or instrument; violent acts producing fractures, bruises, serious wounds; coercion to acts contrary to morals. |
| **Psychological / moral** | Insulting words degrading dignity; threats / intimidation; preventing participation in appropriate activities; defamation of parents to the beneficiary. |
| **Sexual** | Exposure to any sexual activity / arousal for pleasure or harassment; including indecent exposure, watching, exposure to pornographic material. |
| **Neglect** | Non-registration after birth; withholding identity documents; lapses in vaccination follow-up; neglect during meals or clothing-change. |
| **Exploitation** | Material exploitation; involvement in begging or crime; exposure to indecent scenes; allowing underage vehicle operation. |
| **Discrimination** | Discrimination based on ethnic, social, or economic factors. |

**Mandatory reporting**: every public employee + non-profit-sector worker who learns of an abuse case in the course of duty must report to their workplace; the workplace must report immediately to MHRSD or police. **Good-faith reporters are exempted from liability** — explicit whistleblower protection.

24-hour multi-channel reporting infrastructure:

| Channel | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Phone** | **1919** (24/7) |
| **Email** | `1919@hrsd.gov.sa` |
| **Child Support Line** | **116111** |
| **In-person** | Social Protection Units in all regions |
| **Government correspondence** | From security entities, hospitals, schools |

1. **2019 + 2020 promulgation dates.** Documents are 5-6 years old as of 2026. **Verify they remain the active versions** before citing in current pitches; ministerial document refreshes are common.
