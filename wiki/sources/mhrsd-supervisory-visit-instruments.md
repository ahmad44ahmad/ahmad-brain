# MHRSD Supervisory-Visit Instruments

## What this source is

A 13-file Drive folder titled `نماذج تقييم المركز` documenting the **standardised supervisory-visit instruments** used by two MHRSD entities to evaluate comprehensive rehabilitation centers:

- **وكالة تجربة المستفيد** (Beneficiary Experience Wakala) — through its social-supervision units in regional branches.
- **الإدارة العامة للمراجعة الداخلية** (General Administration for Internal Audit).

These are **official forms**, not Ahmad-authored material. The folder is the operational toolkit used during ministerial supervisory visits — the same toolkit applied to [[albaha-center-org]] when supervisory visits land. **Functional importance**: knowing the exact criteria the center is graded against is foundational evidence for [[al-baha-quality-project-2024]]'s "Golden Thread" architecture and for any Basira-ministerial-handover document.

## The strategic-shift statement (load-bearing)

The governance documents in the folder (files 260, 261, 264, 265) carry a **mission-level reframing** of supervisory practice:

> *الانتقال بمنهجية الزيارات الإشرافية من مجرد «رقابة تقليدية» إلى «شراكة تكاملية وتفاعلية» تدعم التطوير والتحسين المستمر، مع التركيز على «الوقوف ميدانياً على التحديات وتقديم الدعم والمساندة»*

This is the **canonical provenance** for [[empowerment-vocabulary]] §Procedural — Governance-shift's *الشراكة التكاملية والتفاعلية* vs *رقابة تقليدية* phrase. The lexicon entry cites JSONL #51; this note is that entry.

The paired *«الزيارة ليست للمحاسبة»* posture (file 265) explicitly de-weaponises supervision — supervisor's job is *تقييم الاحتياجات وتذليل العقبات*, not punishment. Cross-walks with [[ali-alqarni]]'s leadership-thesis framing of supervisory partnership in [[alqarni-leadership-thesis]].

**Stated objectives** (file 264, 261):
- Standardise supervisory procedures + reduce *الاجتهادات الشخصية* (individual discretion).
- Raise efficiency + effectiveness of services delivered.
- Tight oversight of approved-plan execution at branch level.

**Implementation mechanism**: a **scheduled visit matrix** covering all residential branches + supportive social-service units, evaluated via standardised forms with 3-tier scoring (file 260, 261, 257, 258):

| Score | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **3** | موجود (present / fully implemented) |
| **2** | موجود جزئياً (partially present) |
| **1** | لا يوجد (absent / not implemented) |

**Ownership**: the project belongs to وكالة تجربة المستفيد and is executed by social-supervision units in regional ministry branches.

## Three evaluation domains

### 1. Administrative + technical organisation

| Criterion | Sample line items |
|---|---|
| **Leadership + direction** | Display of ministry vision/mission, center objectives, approved org chart |
| **Policies + strategies** | Precautionary procedures (cases / staff / visitors), annual operational plan (objectives, projects, KPIs), activation of approved beneficiary-empowerment + family-guidance plans, regulatory bylaw + procedural manual |
| **Staff monitoring** | Complete staff manifest, attendance/departure records, **enforcement of approved shift periods circular** (physicians, nurses, researchers, etc.), professional-license cards for clinical staff |
| **Case monitoring** | Monthly electronic case statistics, **assigned ministry-staff for camera-monitoring across all facilities**, daily-events + daily-follow-up logs approved by director |
| **Internal-audit additions** | Policy + procedure manual, alignment of job titles with actual work, leave/absence tracking in HR system, **24-hour shift schedule alignment**, complaint-receipt mechanism |

The "**enforcement of approved shift periods**" line item is structurally relevant to [[overtime-grievance-case]] — it confirms that the 8h-shift dispute sits inside an evaluation framework that the ministry actively audits.

### 2. Financial + clothing affairs

- **Financial** — accountable-officer (مأمور عهدة) presence, financial-system application, financial files, dedicated financial committee.
- **Clothing-stock** — electronic listing of all cases with their sizes; scheduled distribution dates; summer + winter clothing on time; festival-occasion clothing; warehouse classification + arrangement; bi-annual inventory minutes.
- **Spending discipline** — application of approved-amounts mechanism (clothing, programs, activities), pre-deadline disbursement tracking, ministry-mandated budget compliance, quality + needs-fit verification per case.

### 3. Operational services

- **Contract management** — bid-package documents available + departments familiar with them, sub-contract documents available, dedicated maintenance + cleaning supervisor.
- **Operational compliance** — **cash-guarantee completeness** (cleaning, maintenance, personal-care, catering) with regulatory withdrawal procedures for contractor violations, contractor-shift-supervision tracking, **monthly electronic invoice submission**.
- **Worker qualification** — contractor-staff qualification + training per contract, uniform compliance.
- **Building + facilities** — building status (gov / leased; excellent / good / dilapidated), identification signage, **disability ramp**, paint condition, cleanliness of facade + offices + restrooms, beneficiary reception hall adequacy, departmental wayfinding signage.

## Pre-visit documentation form

File 259 (`نسخة من نموذج ماقبل زيارة ادارة الدعم.xlsx`) — a **pre-visit self-documentation template** filled by the center BEFORE the supervisor arrives. Structure (truncated in JSONL summary): centers document achievements + operational-contract challenges + resource gaps. Functions as a *negotiated agenda-setting instrument* — gives the center an opportunity to frame the conversation rather than passively receiving evaluation. Worth flagging as a **model Basira can wire into its own self-audit surface** if a "self-attestation before audit" workflow becomes useful.
