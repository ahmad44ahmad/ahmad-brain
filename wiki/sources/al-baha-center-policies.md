**Drive folder:** `1zfL_SKlyny6uoRSFoiz6ML44nP6OK1m5` — تفريغ النصوص لسياسات و الاجراءات و الأدلة الارشادية بالمركز — 24 files.

## Why this folder is priority-2

This is the operational/regulatory reference for the Al-Baha Comprehensive Rehabilitation Center, covering all approved policies, procedures, and guidelines. When writing formal memos, drafting procedure updates, or responding to supervisory feedback, the authoritative institutional language must be drawn from here — not invented or approximated.

## Contents — department modelling

### Medical + rehab departments

| Department | Document scope |
|---|---|
| **Psychology Services Unit** (وحدة الخدمات النفسية) | Psychological assessment, rehabilitation, behaviour modification for persons with disabilities. Contains job descriptions (unit head + psychologist specialist), comprehensive psych-assessment form, treatment plan template (behaviour modification), session documentation — file 4. |
| **Speech & Language** (النطق والتخاطب) | Assessment/diagnosis/treatment of speech, language, voice, and swallow disorders. Speech-specialist defined as health-sector specialist under the Medical Supervisor at the center. Severity-level assessments + individualised treatment plans — file 20. |
| **Swallow-Injury Reduction** (الحد من إصابات البلع — عسر البلع / Dysphagia) | Procedural guide from the General Directorate of Medical Services. Prevents aspiration pneumonia. Defines multi-disciplinary roles (physician, nursing, nutrition, speech, PT, OT, monitor, caregiver) for choking-risk reduction — file 6. |
| **Dental Services** (قسم الأسنان) | Dental supervisor reports to head of Medical Services. Supervises oral-health curative + preventive programs, needs assessment, performance evaluation, compliance with quality + infection-control programs — file 15. |
| **Nursing** | (per Gemini summary, file listed but detail deferred) |

### Non-clinical / administrative

| Area | Documents |
|---|---|
| **Social Activities** (الأنشطة الاجتماعية) | Annual plan form (model 1), activity documentation form (model 2), activities-section follow-up form (model 3) — links activity objectives to operational plan + budget line items. Internal + external participants logged — file 9. |
