## PT — newly enumerated (Ahmad's department)

The four weighted PT KPIs, all targeting 100%:

| KPI (AR) | English gloss | Formula | Weight | Frequency |
|---|---|---|---:|---|
| الحالات المتلقية لخدمات تأهيل طبي بوزارة الصحة | Cases receiving MoH medical-rehab services | received / referred × 100 | 1% | quarterly cumulative |
| الجبائر المؤمَّنة من وزارة الصحة | Splints secured from MoH | secured / requested × 100 | 1% | quarterly cumulative |
| المستفيدين المُقَيَّمين ولديهم خطة علاجية | Beneficiaries assessed with active plan | assessed-with-plan / referred × 100 | **2%** | monthly stage |
| الجلسات العلاجية المُقَدَّمة | Therapy sessions delivered | (sessions/4) / cases-with-plan × 100 | **2%** | monthly stage |

**Operational read:** PT's two highest-weight items are *coverage* metrics (assessment + delivery) inside the center. The two lower-weight items are *external-coordination* (MoH referrals and splint procurement) — these depend on the supplier (وزارة الصحة) and aren't fully under PT's control. Ahmad's role as PT supervisor is most rewarded by raising the two coverage rates, both worth 2% each.

## Critique inheritance

All three structural concerns from [[mhrsd-2025-kpi-portfolio]] persist in the now-complete scorecard:

1. **Zero-event under-reporting anti-pattern.** Most weighted single KPI is *الإصابات الجسيمة* at 10%, target 0%. Combined with *حالات التقييد* (4%) and *الإصابات البسيطة والمتوسطة* (4%, target <10%), three of the seven medical KPIs reward incident-suppression rather than incident-prevention.
2. **Medical-model framing throughout.** Every department's KPIs are clinical-throughput metrics. No beneficiary-self-report, no dignity-preservation metric, no community-integration outcome. Documented in [[social-handicap-compass]] as a tension Ahmad's empowerment frame must hold against.
3. **Cumulative vs stage aggregation mix.** Mostly *مرحلي* (per-period stage). Five KPIs are *تراكمي* (cumulative quarterly or semi-annual): periodic labs, primary-care visits, specialist clinics, MoH PT services, splints, dental visits, dental plans. Cumulative metrics resist tactical end-of-quarter scrambles; stage metrics show monthly volatility.

## Worked sums

- **Direct + indirect Medical/Nursing/Pharmacy = 67%** (the 2025 portfolio page's prior bound).
- **Therapy stack (PT + Speech + Psych + Nutrition + Dental) = 33%** (this page's contribution).
- **PT alone = 6%** — modest individual weight, but PT outcomes feed Nutrition (مؤشر النحافة), Nursing (mobility-related pressure ulcers, fall injuries), and Medical (fall-injury count) indirectly. Real PT influence on the scorecard is larger than the headline 6%.

- Ahmad's name in the field-visit form as *رئيس قسم الجودة* (recorded in the framework page) means he is also accountable for documentation/records-keeping that feeds *every* KPI's data source.
