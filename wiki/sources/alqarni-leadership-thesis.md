# Al-Qarni Leadership Thesis Folder

## What this source is

A Drive folder titled `علي القرني` containing a strategic dossier Ahmad authored / curated **for** Ali Al-Qarni, then-director of مركز التأهيل الشامل بالباحة (took directorship 17/4/1444 H ≈ 2022-11-12). Folder volume: 80+ files, Gemini reviewed 60. Working horizon: late 2024 leading to a planned visit by **deputy minister Ismail Al-Ghamdi** (per file 2/6).

This is **Ahmad-authored / Ahmad-curated** working material — not a Gemini synthesis. The dossier is the document trail for one of Ahmad's "soft-power" institutional efforts: building a credible leadership-promotion case for his manager.

**Status flag.** [[ali-alqarni]] notes that Al-Qarni stepped back Q1 2026. This folder is therefore a **historical artefact** — preserved here as provenance for the *نموذج أولي استراتيجي* phrase in [[empowerment-vocabulary]] and as evidence that the empowerment-pivot framing of Al-Qarni's leadership was Ahmad's authorship. Do not propose live escalation paths anchored on Al-Qarni from this folder.

## Layer 1 — The leadership-thesis dossier

The strategic core: a "leadership thesis" packaging Al-Qarni's management as **«نموذج أولي استراتيجي»** (strategic prototype) for the empowerment-economy paradigm. Files 6, 8, 31 carry the thesis structure.

**Framing axis** (verbatim phrasing surfaced in folder):

> *"الانتقال من «منظومة الرعاية التفاعلية» إلى «منظومة التمكين الاستباقي»"*

— the cleanest critique↔vision pair in [[empowerment-vocabulary]] §Vision. The folder is the **provenance source** for that lexicon entry; the lexicon's claim that the phrasing came from "the Al-Qarni leadership-thesis file" anchors here.

The thesis explicitly invokes [[empowerment-vocabulary]] §Critique vocabulary as the contrast field:

- *مؤسسات كلية* (Total Institutions, Goffman) — names what reactive-care administrations become.
- *طغيان المؤشرات الكمية* — names the KPI pathology, with *صفر حوادث* as canonical anti-pattern.
- *صناعة الاعتمادية* — names the output the pathology produces.

**Documented Al-Baha achievements** (1444 H — 2024 G, files 9 + 17):

- Quarterly reporting alignment with ministry vision targets.
- Beneficiaries winning national-games medals (table tennis).
- Resident-safety + aspiration-prevention training rolled out by the medical department, year-round training calendar.
- Four community-cooperation MoUs signed with external entities for future initiatives + services.

**Future-aspirations layer** (file 6): *"مشروع المساعد لقطاع التنمية"* — the file argues Al-Baha's model offers a practical solution to structural gaps in care/rehabilitation philosophy, scalable to lead the Development Sector at Qassim region or kingdom-wide.

**Personal-context note** (file 2): the dossier observes Al-Qarni **had not received promotion or privileges** despite his recorded achievements while peers had been advanced. The file alludes to the prior GM (now reportedly imprisoned in a corruption case) suppressing visible-performer staff. The personal-context section also documents a planned visit by Ismail Al-Ghamdi (deputy minister, regional-origin من أهل المنطقة) where Ahmad proposed a **"podcast" / innovative human-narrative format** for the achievement file rather than a flat formal text.

## Layer 2 — Investigation R-263555 defense file

The folder contains a complete strategic-defense template for an administrative investigation (likely Al-Qarni or a colleague). **Do not conflate with [[overtime-grievance-case]]** — this is a separate matter (case ID R-263555, complaint-closure procedure issue).

**Three-pillar defense methodology** (files 18, 19, 20, 22, 30):

| Layer | Argument | Reference |
|---|---|---|
| **1. Procedural — prescription (تقادم)** | Incident date 1440/03/24 H. Investigation start 1446/08/18 H. Gap >6 years. Demand: apply 2-year prescription period from new 1443 H disciplinary-conduct system, per *مبدأ القانون الأصلح للمتهم* (most-favourable-law-to-the-accused principle). Sufficient on its own to dismiss. | files 18, 22, 30 |
| **2. Substantive — professional safety** | Charge: "insufficient diligence" in handling repeat complaint R-263555. Counter: closure was a textbook *Case Management* application — there was an **active file** for the beneficiary, closure of duplicates is sound resource-discipline. | files 20, 22, 30 |
| **3. Element — absence of harm** | Beneficiary suffered no damage; case follow-up continued under the active file. **Harm is a constitutive element of administrative violations** — its absence collapses the charge. | files 18, 22, 30 |

Procedural rights asserted (files 3, 4, 5): right to written defense, right to inspect investigation file, right to rebuttal.

**Vault-significance.** This 3-pillar framework is directly transferable to other admin-defense scenarios. The template's lexicon (تقادم, القانون الأصلح للمتهم, ركن الضرر) extends [[empowerment-vocabulary]] §Procedural-grievance domain with technical-legal vocabulary the existing empowerment-grievance set (التزوير المعنوي, اغتصاب صلاحية, الإكراه الإداري) doesn't fully cover. Worth recording as a methodology even though no direct overlap with the overtime-grievance case.
