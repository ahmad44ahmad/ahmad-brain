# MHRSD 2024 KPI Templates — Social Section

## What this page adds

The 2025 master scorecard ([[mhrsd-2025-kpi-master-scorecard]]) is **medical-side only** — Medical, Nursing, Pharmacy, PT, Speech, Psychological, Nutrition, Dental. **There is no social-section bucket in that scorecard.** The social section runs on a **separate KPI track** captured in this 2024 form-template, pulled forward to 2025 unchanged.

This page enumerates that parallel track.

## Five domains, fourteen sub-KPIs

| # | Domain (AR) | English gloss | Sub-KPIs | Polarity |
|---|---|---|---:|---|
| 1 | تنمية المهارات | Skill development | 3 | + |
| 2 | البرامج والأنشطة الاجتماعية | Social programs + activities | 4 | + |
| 3 | التمكين الوظيفي | Vocational empowerment | 2 | + |
| 4 | الإرشاد الأسري | Family guidance | 3 | + |
| 5 | الرعاية اللاحقة + الرضا | After-care + satisfaction | 2 | + |
| | | **TOTAL** | **14** | — |

## Domain detail

### 1. Skill development (تنمية المهارات)

| Sub-KPI | What it measures |
|---|---|
| % training-plans executed | Out of approved center training plans |
| % beneficiaries trained on independence + social skills | Eating, drinking, toileting, self-reliance — independent living foundations |
| % families participating in beneficiary-training programs | Family inclusion in skill-development |

**Operational read:** the *family-participation* sub-KPI is structurally significant — it forces the center to engage the family unit in training, not just the beneficiary. Aligned with social-handicap-compass framing.

### 2. Social programs + activities (البرامج والأنشطة)

| Sub-KPI | What it measures |
|---|---|
| % internal social activities executed | Center-internal events |
| % external social activities executed | Community-facing events (per the 2025 framework, target 4/month) |
| % families participating in social activities | Family inclusion in events |
| % external bodies participating in social activities | Community-organization partnership |

**Cross-link to 2025 framework page:** *"Annual target: 192 activities/year = 12 internal + 4 external per month"* — this domain operationalizes that target.

### 3. Vocational empowerment (التمكين الوظيفي)

| Sub-KPI | What it measures | Formula |
|---|---|---|
| % empowered + employed | Beneficiaries placed in jobs/crafts | (placed / targeted) × 100 |
| % retention on the job | Continuity in employment | (still employed / placed) × 100 |

**Operational read:** this is the *outcome metric closest to genuine empowerment* in the entire MHRSD KPI surface. Sustained employment is the strongest single signal of successful rehabilitation. Two sub-KPIs only because the upstream pipeline (matching, placement, job coaching) is captured under skill-development and family-guidance domains.

### 4. Family guidance (الإرشاد الأسري)

| Sub-KPI | What it measures |
|---|---|
| % family-guidance plans executed + approved | Per-family written plan |
| % family-guidance programs executed + approved | Per-family training program (group) |
| % beneficiaries transitioned from rehab center to community services | **The deinstitutionalization metric** |

**The transition metric is the most empowerment-aligned KPI in the whole MHRSD surface.** "% beneficiaries who left the rehab center for community services" directly measures the institutional → community pipeline. Required supporting documents include *نماذج طي القيد* (deregistration forms).

### 5. After-care + satisfaction (الرعاية اللاحقة + الرضا)

| Sub-KPI | What it measures |
|---|---|
| % after-care beneficiaries (post-deregistration) | Continuity of support after leaving the center |
| % beneficiary + family satisfaction | Self-reported (this is the *only* self-report metric in the entire 2024+2025 surface) |

**Notable:** the satisfaction sub-KPI is the **only beneficiary-self-report metric** in the entire 2024-2025 KPI inventory (medical-side scorecard + social-section templates combined). The 2026 social-homes instrument also lacks self-report (staff records depression/pain/communication, not beneficiaries themselves). This sub-KPI is structurally important and worth surfacing in any [[basira]] argument about beneficiary voice.

## Position in the wider scorecard

```

│   ├── Psychological 8%

│   └── Dental 5%
│
└── Social-section parallel track (this page) → social-services head accountability
    ├── Skill development (3 sub-KPIs)
    ├── Programs + activities (4 sub-KPIs, 192/year target)
    ├── Vocational empowerment (2 sub-KPIs) ← strongest empowerment signal
    ├── Family guidance (3 sub-KPIs) ← includes deinstitutionalization metric
    └── After-care + satisfaction (2 sub-KPIs) ← only self-report metric
```

## Critique inheritance

The structural critiques from [[mhrsd-2025-kpi-master-scorecard]] are **less applicable here** — this is the empowerment-aligned half of the surface:

| Critique | Medical-side scorecard | Social-section templates |
|---|---|---|
| Zero-event under-reporting | Heavy (3 of 7 medical KPIs) | Absent — all 14 sub-KPIs are positive-polarity |
| Medical-model framing | Dominant | Inverted — empowerment + community framing |
| No beneficiary self-report | Confirmed | **Partially addressed** by sub-KPI 5.2 (satisfaction) |

**Implication for [[basira]]:** when arguing the empowerment thesis to ministry leadership, the social-section KPI track is **already on the empowerment side** structurally. The argument is not "create new KPIs" but "shift weight + visibility from medical-side to social-section." The infrastructure exists.

## Carry-over signal

This sheet — designed for 2024 — was pulled into 2025 **unchanged**. Two readings:

1. **Stable design** — the social-section KPIs work; nothing to revise.
2. **Lagged revision** — the medical-side scorecard got an explicit 2025 weighted update (see [[mhrsd-2025-kpi-portfolio]] تعميم 28597); the social side did not get the equivalent ministerial refresh.

Reading 2 is more likely given the 2026 social-homes instrument shows the ministry IS revising social-side measurement just for a different scope (homes vs. rehab centers).
