# Community Integration Project

## What this source is

An 18-file Drive folder titled `مشروع الدمج المجتمعي كامل` documenting **وكالة التأهيل والتوجيه الاجتماعي**'s strategic transition project — moving beneficiaries from residential rehabilitation centers (`الرعاية الإيوائية`) into community-based settings + alternative service packages.

This is **official MHRSD strategic material**, not Ahmad-authored. Vault-significance: documents the **operational implementation of the empowerment-pivot** at the ministerial-program level — the same pivot that anchors [[empowerment-thesis-corpus]] + [[empowerment-vocabulary]], operationalised here as a concrete migration pathway with KPIs.

## CRPD Article 19 anchoring

The project's stated foundation is the **United Nations Convention on the Rights of Persons with Disabilities (CRPD), specifically Article 19** (right to live independently in the community). This makes the project an MHRSD-side execution of Saudi CRPD ratification commitments — concrete evidence that the social-rights model is operationalised inside the ministry, not just rhetorically endorsed.

The folder also invokes **religious anchoring** — workshop materials cite *«ليس على الأعمى حرج ولا على الأعرج حرج ولا على المريض حرج»* (Quranic verse on PWD inclusion). This is the same indigenous-Islamic-anchor pattern documented in [[empowerment-vocabulary]] §"External inheritances → Saudi Islamic register".

## Strategic vision (3 axes)

**Definition of integration**: an *«أسلوب اجتماعي وأخلاقي»* (social and ethical approach) that achieves equality and justice for PWDs, eliminates negative perception, and provides the conditions for successful community reintegration.

**Core objective**: *«تحقيق الدمج الاجتماعي الكامل للأشخاص ذوي الإعاقة داخل مجتمعهم المحلي»* with full equality of rights and freedoms — direct CRPD Article 19 implementation.

**Therapeutic framing**: explicitly post-isolation. Files 2 + 3 frame integration as the historical-progression beyond *«مرحلة العزلة»* (the isolation era) toward viewing PWDs as *«جزء لا يتجزأ من المجتمع»* — directly inverts the *مؤسسات كلية* (Total Institutions, Goffman) critique in [[empowerment-vocabulary]].

## Operational structure

The Social Department is the project's executing engine. Three sub-departments each carry a distinct mandate:

| Sub-department | Mandate |
|---|---|
| **التأهيل المجتمعي** (Community Rehabilitation) | Training programs supporting integration. Operational plan stages: data gathering + case study → planning → selection + assignment → execution → monitoring + evaluation → escalation to relevant authorities. |
| **المتابعة الاجتماعية** (Social Follow-up) | Post-integration follow-up of transitioned cases (Form 27). |
| **الإرشاد الأسري** (Family Guidance) | Family preparation for accepting integration + participation in achieving "happiness, comfort, family safety". Family Guidance Team + Head of Social Services attendance mandatory in workshops. |

A separate ministerial circular activated the Social-Services Department at center level + standardised procedures + forms — explicit performance-measurement KPIs introduced for social-service quality.

## KPIs (load-bearing)

Two named performance indicators:

| KPI | Code | Polarity | Definition |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Residential → non-residential transition rate** | ت-1-1 | **Positive** | Rate of cases exiting comprehensive rehab centers vs total resident count. **Baseline (2019): 2%.** Goal: increase to reflect integration success. |
| **Family-visit frequency** | ت-2-1 | **Negative** | Number of cases whose families visit (in-person + video + phone) **fewer than 4 times/month**. Lower negative-count = better. |

The 2% 2019 baseline is the **single most concrete data point in the integration project's documentation** — gives "before" measurement for any "after" claim. Worth highlighting in any Basira-pitch quantification of social-empowerment progress.

## 3-phase migration plan

| Phase | Population | Destination |
|---|---|---|
| **1** | Cases meeting community-integration eligibility criteria, with families | Family-based reintegration |
| **2** | Cases needing alternative-care settings | Home-care + Social-services package |
| **3** | All other cases | Alternative service packages (day-care, home health, etc.) |

**Alternative service mix** named in folder: day-care, home-care (under the Home Social Care Program), long-term residential care (for cases unable to reintegrate), vocational rehabilitation, home health, PWD homes (دور ذوي الإعاقة).

## Implementation instruments

- **Form 27 — متابعة المستفيد (الرعاية اللاحقة)** — post-integration tracking template; records visit purpose, date, alternative services accessed, observations.
- **Annual Social Activities Plan (Form 1)** — community-rehabilitation programs across the year. Examples: National Civil Defence Day (سلامتك تهمنا), Young Trader (التاجر الصغير, sales / commerce skills), Personal Grooming Skills.
- **Community Partnerships** — MoU framework with profit + non-profit sectors for: home accessibility modifications, transportation, training programs, assistive devices.

## V4 operationalization (2026-06-04)

In the V4 rebuild ([[basira-v4-rebuild]]) this de-institutionalisation strategy is the central empowerment cross-service trigger. The CRPD de-institutionalisation guide (`KB: harvest/14-a`) supplies the operational layer this folder's summary lacked:

- **The §5 discharge-routing taxonomy** — each beneficiary is classified into one of ~7 source classes (stay / refer / MoH-extended / MoH-home / alternative-services / family-supported / family-ready), and **each class emits a destination + a hard gating precondition**. The axis is social situation + family capability + service availability, **never diagnosis**. This is the authoritative source behind V4's `integrationCategory` enum (the 8-enum — family-reintegration / remain / alternative-care / medical / education / home-care / orphan / elderly — reconciles to this 7-class taxonomy; recover-vs-redefine is a genuinely open decision).
- **The طي القيد discharge-interlock** — file-closure (de-registration) is BLOCKED until cross-service preconditions are met (home adapted + MoH home-care confirmed + family trained). It is **tri-state**, not binary: the person physically moves home with the file OPEN and after-care running; only file-CLOSURE is held. This is the de-institutionalisation analogue of the dental→soft-diet wire — a KNOW event (home/family readiness) gating a SERVE action (file-closure) in another service. V4 ships it as an interlock with its negative case (see [[basira-v4-cross-service-triggers]]).

The legal trigger is المادة 25 of اللائحة الأساسية لبرامج المعاقين (قرار مجلس الوزراء 34, 1400هـ); the CRPD anchoring (Arts 7/19/23) above is the rights basis.
