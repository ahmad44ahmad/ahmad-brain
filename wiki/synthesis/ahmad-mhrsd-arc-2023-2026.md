## Evidence layers (read every date with its source)

The timeline below fuses two archives of different shape. **Always read a date with its layer** — the layer tells
you how strong the evidence is:

- **[deep]** — full message body, from [[pst-archive-2023-2026]] (2025-03 → 2026-03). Highest confidence; the thread's actual content is known.
- **[envelope]** — header/subject metadata only, from [[pst-mailbox-hrsd-merged]] (2023-11 → 2024, and pre-2023 drafts). The email's existence + date are known; its content is not extracted.
- **[gap]** — not in any local archive (pre-2023 active inbox/sent). Flagged for a future older-archive harvest.

**The zero-2024 trap:** the deep archive has no 2024 at all — an *export boundary*, not a quiet year. 2024 was in
fact Ahmad's most active documented year (1,635 messages [envelope]). Never narrate 2024 as silent.
