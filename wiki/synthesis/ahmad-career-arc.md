# Ahmad's MHRSD Career Arc

Earlier vault and memory material flattened Ahmad's role to *"PT specialist, Al-Baha rehab centre."* He corrected this explicitly on 2026-04-21: the different titles and specialties are **chronological progression and accumulated expertise on a single ~14-year career arc**, not competing identities. Any document that presents him as "just a PT specialist" is structurally incomplete and will underperform against the nomination and Basira narratives.
