## What it is, in one paragraph

The single dominant thread of Ahmad's 2025-06 → 2026-03 mailbox: the biometric attendance system (نظام البصمة)
flagged roughly **34 absence-days (Jun-2025 → Jan-2026)**, of which about **14 were deducted (~12,000+ SAR)**. Ahmad
contests them on two grounds — **(1) the fingerprint system was at fault** (device failures, mis-placement on the
التسكين/placement roster), and **(2) full salary was paid for the periods, which he argues proves no real absence
occurred.** The dispute escalated from routine "explain your absence" demands into a formal administrative grievance,
a legal-consultation request, and finally an OPEN Khadamati incident **`INC0658186` (2026-03-01)** requesting reversal.
As of the latest record the ready-to-paste complaint text is drafted; **the final step is to submit it.**

should be stopped** — citing a **Riyadh-branch fingerprint-stoppage circular** and a **vice-minister risk-register
approval of the contact-fingerprint risk**. This folds his professional expertise into his personal defence: the
same competence that makes him an IPC authority is deployed against the system deducting his salary. It is also why
this grievance is one of the **three converging dossiers** (attendance + building-safety + IPC-fingerprint) that
share a 9-item evidence bundle — see [[ahmad-mhrsd-arc-2023-2026]] §converging-dossiers.
