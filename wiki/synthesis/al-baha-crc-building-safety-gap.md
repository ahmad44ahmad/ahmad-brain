# Al-Baha CRC Building-Safety Gap

## The non-conformance

A residential rehabilitation center operating under a "Safety First" policy has **no automatic fire-suppression system and no civil-defense certificate** — in a facility whose residents, by definition, **cannot self-rescue**. That is the gap. What makes it load-bearing is not the finding itself but that **three independent document trails record the same thing across four years** — it is corroborated, quantified, and unresolved, not a one-off observation.

## The multi-year arc — three independent records

| When | Record (independent source) | What it found |
|---|---|---|
| **2022** | Civil-defense-driven inspection (Center-Assets harvest → [[albaha-center-facility-plant]]) | No diesel fire pump; fire-alarm panel defects (one dead). |
| **June 2024** | COPE risk audit (QMS harvest) | Building classed **high-risk, no sprinklers** — COPE = Construction / Occupancy / Protection / Exposure. |
| **Nov 2025** | QMS internal audit ([[al-baha-crc-quality-management-system]]) | Logged as a **Major Non-Conformance (NCR-001)**. |
| **Dec 2025** | QMS executive plan | → **PRJ-SAF** fire-suppression project (~150–300K SAR, target 30 Mar 2026). |
| **2026** | Ministry risk-team escalation ([[pst-mailbox-hrsd]]) | Escalated upward (specifics in the email archive — pointer, not reproduced here). |

Three independent harvests (QMS corpus, physical-plant archive, email escalation) converging on the same finding is what turns "an observation" into a **documented institutional gap with a paper trail**.

## Why it cannot be engineered away by policy alone

The center is a hybrid residential + clinical facility; its severe-mobility and cognitive-impairment residents cannot evacuate on a standard 2–3 minute target — which [[999-albaha-qms-bcp]]'s Amplified-Risk Profile explicitly rejects as unrealistic for this population. So the gap is not closable by drill frequency; it needs the engineered control (automatic suppression + areas of refuge + PEEPs) that PRJ-SAF and the SAR 6.26M أمان مستدام tender ([[aman-mustadam-initiative]]) are scoped to deliver. This is the «هندسة» (engineer-the-hazard-out) posture, not a training fix.

## Why it matters

The building-safety escalation is one of the differentiators the [[nomination-portfolio]] draws on (via [[safety-risk-portfolio]] and [[awad-alshahri-safety]], who holds the 23-observation civil-defense escalation). This note is the cross-harvest narrative that ties the plant baseline, the QMS non-conformance, and the ministry escalation into one story — the credible, triangulated form of "the building isn't safe yet, and here is the four-year paper trail proving it was known and acted on."
