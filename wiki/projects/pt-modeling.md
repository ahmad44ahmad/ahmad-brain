# PT Services Modelling — MHRSD 36 Centres

## What this project is

Ahmad was selected by MHRSD to propose improvements to the existing **"نمذجة خدمات العلاج الطبيعي"** — the official operational manual that governs how PT departments function across the 36 Comprehensive Rehabilitation Centres nationwide. (*Verification pending:* Ahmad has recently referenced "38 centres" in conversation; the count to use in any formal deliverable must be confirmed first.)

**What "نمذجة" means here:** standardisation / templating of a service — i.e. an operational policy manual, not academic research and not strategic reform. The deliverable is a government SOP handbook with the familiar structure: introduction, objectives, organisational structure, job descriptions, the fourteen `physiopp 001–014` procedures, forms, equipment, references.

**Why now:** the existing document is structurally sound but outdated. Since its issuance:

- The ministry has restructured (الخدمات الطبية → إدارة سلامة المستفيدين; وكالة التأهيل → وكالة تجربة المستفيد).
- The Disability Rights Law 1445H has been enacted.
- SCFHS has published a new professional-classification framework.
- The Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL) is in force.

The work is therefore update-in-place — *"things that changed since issuance"* — not demolition-and-rewrite.

## Deliverables pipeline (strict order)

1. **Three analytical studies** — brainstorming / analytical foundation. **Not the final deliverable.**
   - Study 1 — Regulatory & Professional Compliance Gap Analysis.
   - Study 2 — Best Practices & Benchmarking.
   - Study 3 — Development Proposals.
2. **Proposals memo** — 2–3-page formal memo to إدارة سلامة المستفيدين. Only after the three studies are aligned.
3. **Updated modelling document** — the actual manual. Same structure, same governmental Arabic, same format as the original (40–60 pages). Only after the proposals memo is approved.

Jumping ahead (e.g., writing the manual before the memo, or writing the memo before the studies converge) is the failure mode that aborted the 2026-04-09 session.

## The nine non-negotiable rules

Every deliverable is audited against these by the `pt-modeling-qa` skill. **Scope first, then quality** — scope violations cannot be rescued by polish.

1. **PT as a service, not as a workforce.** The document governs the *service*, not the employees, not specific centres, not center-level governance.
2. **Zero CBAHI.** MHRSD is institutionally independent of MOH. CBAHI is not the regulator here and must not appear as a binding standard.
3. **Zero personal initiatives.** Tamkeen, Masarrah, Basira — none of Ahmad's cross-cutting initiatives belong inside the official modelling document. Cross-pollinating vocabulary breaks both the governance scope and the institutional voice.
4. **General and durable.** The manual must work for any centre, any service model (residential, day-care, home-based, post-privatisation) for a 5+-year horizon.
5. **Governmental Arabic register.** يتم / يُحدد / يلتزم constructions. Not AI-academic. Not personal. Not essayistic. Mirror the original document's voice.
6. **Do not criticise the original authors.** Frame gaps as *"things that changed since issuance"*, never as *"the original was wrong"*.
7. **QA after every step, scope-first.** The `pt-modeling-qa` skill runs after every meaningful change. Never drop it after the corrections phase.
8. **Build on what exists.** The operational skeleton — policies `physiopp 001–014` — is structurally sound. Update, do not demolish.
9. **Do not overload.** Frontline government employees need clear, practical procedures. No philosophical discussions, no speculative sections, no ornamental prose.

The operational behaviour rules sit in memory at `feedback_pt_project_rules.md`; this hub holds the project-scope content. The two are paired.

## Source-document structure (preserve in the rewrite)

The rewrite must retain every one of these sections, in this order:

- Title page.
- Table of contents.
- Introduction.
- Eight objectives.
- Organisational structure.
- Job descriptions — Head, Senior Specialist, Specialist, Technician, Assistant.
- Fourteen procedures (`physiopp 001–014`).
- Forms.
- Equipment.
- References.

Reorganising the skeleton is out of scope — even if an improved ordering exists, shipping the document under a different structure fails Rule 6 (treats the original as wrong) and Rule 9 (unnecessary cognitive load for frontline users).
